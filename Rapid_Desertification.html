Rapid Desertification is an anomaly event chain triggered by investigating the anomaly with the same name, which can only appear on Dry worlds.

Alien Fauna Identified
We have found life on [anomaly planet name]. Vast non-sapient herds of small mammals roam across the entire planetary surface.

Their population, labeled 'desertlings' by our scientists, is increasing at an alarming rate. The planet is already beyond its capacity, and the ecosystem is deteriorating rapidly.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Not Gestalt Consciousness
 Is triggered only by:

Investigating the Rapid Desertification anomaly

Investigate why this is happening now.
  • Issue Special Project: Encumbered Ecosystem
Best not intervene.

Invasive Species
While studying the desertlings on [anomaly planet name] our scientists have made a pivotal observation. Despite the ubiquitous nature of the species, there is a striking absence of matching fossil records. These ecological interlopers have caused extreme environmental damage over a relatively short time period, and were most likely transported here by an outside actor.

The pressing need for intervention has become abundantly clear. Without action, these creatures will destroy what remains of this planet's natural ecosystem.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Alien Fauna Identified
 Is triggered only by:

Finishing the Encumbered Ecosystem special project

We will adopt as many critters as possible.
  • Alien Domestication modifier added to every colony for 10 years, giving the following effects:
    • Society from Jobs +10%
    • Housing −5%
Enact animal population controls.
  • Issue Special Project: Population Control
We'll take some animals as livestock.
  • Every colony gets 2 pre-sapient species with Desert Preference, Delicious, and Unintelligent traits
Exterminate the beasts.

Extinction Event
"We have received news that [anomaly planet name] has succumbed to the infestation of the exponentially breeding mammals. Deep silence envelops the now barren landscape, where all traces of vegetation have been devoured down to their roots.

The omnivorous mammals themselves lie still, either perished from starvation, or eaten by their kin as the famine progressed.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Alien Fauna Identified
  • Did not finish the Encumbered Ecosystem special project
 Mean time to happen:

18 months

OK
  • The planet where the anomaly was found becomes a Barren World
  • Colony is destroyed if the planet was colonized

Nature's Rebirth
After an arduous effort to sterilize and genetically alter the desertlings on [anomaly planet name], their population has finally been reduced to a sustainable level.

Without the overwhelming amount of grazers, a delicate equilibrium has emerged. Nature is rapidly restoring itself, as fresh patches of grass begin to sprout over the once barren desert.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Invasive Species
 Is triggered only by:

Finishing the Population Control special project

A hidden paradise.
  • The planet where the anomaly was found gains the Lush modifier and the Bountiful Plains and Green Hills planetary features

National Treasures
When we opened our planets to the desertlings, no one could have anticipated the results. The critters have adapted seamlessly into our way of life, becoming beloved companions in households all across our territory.

Their rapid integration has led to speculation that these creatures must at once time have been domesticated. Whatever the cause of their innate friendliness, the rumor only adds to the fascination surrounding these extraordinary creatures.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Invasive Species
  • 60% chance
 Is triggered only by:

Alien Domestication modifiers expire

In a way, they saved us.
  • Unlikely Bond modifier added for 5 years, giving the following effects:
    • Happiness +10%
    • Amenities Pop Amenities Usage −20%
  • The Troublesome Critters event cannot happen

Red Desert
Finally, the last of the rodents lie dead on [anomaly planet name]. The sand itself has been tinted red by our massive effort to save the planet from its infestation, but already new green buds have begun to sprout. We were just in time.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Invasive Species
  • Did not gain any reward
 Mean time to happen:

6 months

It had to be done.
  • Food Gained: 500
  • The planet where the anomaly was found gains the Red Desert planetary feature
  • The Troublesome Critters event cannot happen

Troublesome Critters
Dire news has reached us. A few desertlings have broken free during transportation, forming a rapidly expanding community in the wild. The situation must be handled swiftly to avoid an environmental collapse.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Alien Fauna Identified
  • Clonized the planet where the anomaly was found
  • Did not get the Nature's Rebirth, National Treasures or Red Desert events
 Mean time to happen:

10 years

Hunt the beasts down!
  • The planet where the anomaly was found gains the Invasive Fauna planetary feature
Build a walled reserve.
  • −1000
  • The planet where the anomaly was found gains the Alien Safari planetary feature

Mammalian Population Stabilized
We have been moderately successful in curbing the rampant desertling infestation. Regrettably, we have been unable to eradicate the elusive beasts. It is crucial to continue monitoring the situation, as it could reach a tipping point at any moment.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Troublesome Critters
  • The planet where the anomaly was found has the Invasive Fauna planetary feature
  • 50% chance
 Mean time to happen:

6 months

The hunt continues.
  • Food Gained: 1000

Uncontrollable Infestation
Our attempt to control the infestation of desertlings has been rendered futile. The voracious creatures have already burrowed deep, expanding their large colonies faster than we can exterminate them. The battle is lost unless we allow our military to use their vast arsenal of explosives and noxious gasses, a course of action which would risk the well-being of everyone on the surface.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Troublesome Critters
  • The planet where the anomaly was found has the Invasive Fauna planetary feature
  • 50% chance
 Mean time to happen:

6 months

The animals are here to stay...
  • Alien Infestation modifier added to the planet where the anomaly was found for 5 years, giving the following effects: Habitability −20%
We will NOT lose a war to mere rodents.
  • Extreme Extermination modifier added to the planet where the anomaly was found for 1 year, giving the following effects:
    • Army Cost −50%
    • Army Build Speed +100%
    • [1] Pop Growth Reduction −100%
    • Automatic Resettlment Destination Chance −100%
  1. ↑ Obsolete icon code, use "pop growth speed"
