This template can be use to display an ingame used icon with the according descrition in a framed box.

Example

{{desc|Faction icons imperialists|Imperialist Factions desire dominance over other Empires. They like Rivalries and conquering other Empires, and dislike relying on others.}}

will display:

Imperialist Factions desire dominance over other Empires. They like Rivalries and conquering other Empires, and dislike relying on others.
