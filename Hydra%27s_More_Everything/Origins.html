Hydra's More Everything/Origins

Most origins in Hydra's More Everything come from Hydra's More origins.

These origins vary in complexity and power level, and some can be used by ai empires.

None can be used by pre-ftl empires.

Some origins uses features from DLC and is therefore locked behind the appropriate DLC(s).
