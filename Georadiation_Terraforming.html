Georadiation Terraforming is an event chain that will start for the empire that fully excavates the Subterranean Network archaeological site.

Gravitational Fuel
We have successfully uncovered another damaged terraforming device on [special project planet name]. Unlike the original construct, this one has retained some of its fuel source for study, revealing Dark Matter as the driving force of its radiation output.

The device accelerates growth of targeted resources by metastasizing key molecular bonds. This promotes their development at the expense of the surrounding biosphere's cellular integrity.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Excavated the Subterranean Network archaeological site
 Is triggered only by:

Finishing the first Signal Trace special project

Ramarkable.
  • Dark Matter Gained: 100-1000
  • Engineering Research Gained: 250-100000
  • Society Research Gained: 250-100000
  • Planet where the special project took place gains a deposit of +1 Dark Matter
  • If both special projects are completed issues the Undetonated Device special project

Stimulated Radiation
We have successfully uncovered another damaged terraforming device on [special project planet name], encased in crystal growths caused by a misfire from a broken refractor array.

Through close observation of the apparatus, we have identified the methods used for focused radiation targeting. By utilizing controlled modulating feedback loops, the construct is able to directly target and stimulate the growth of a pre-determined resource.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Excavated the Subterranean Network archaeological site
 Is triggered only by:

Finishing the second Signal Trace special project

Ramarkable.
  • Rare Crystals Gained: 100-1000
  • Engineering Research Gained: 250-100000
  • Society Research Gained: 250-100000
  • Planet where the special project took place gains a deposit of +2 Rare Crystals
  • If both special projects are completed issues the Undetonated Device special project

Undetonated Device
We have successfully located an intact fourth device, inactive on the surface of [special project asteroid name]. Analyzing its control architecture would give us the final knowledge needed to replicate this technology.

The potential ramifications of freely deploying such technology on a galactic scale raise significant questions.

Perhaps it would be better if the potential risk these devices pose was removed from the galaxy.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Gravitational Fuel
  • Stimulated Radiation
 Is triggered only by:

Finishing the second Undetonated Device special project

Study the device.
  • Dark Matter Gained: 100-1000
  • Rare Crystals Gained: 100-1000
  • Engineering Research Gained: 250-100000
  • Society Research Gained: 250-100000
  • If the Ancient Relics DLC is installed gain 100-1000 Minor Artifacts
  • Issue Special Project: Device Analysis
The risks outweigh the rewards, destroy the device.
  • Dark Matter Gained: 100-1000
  • Rare Crystals Gained: 100-1000
  • Engineering Research Gained: 250-100000
  • Society Research Gained: 250-100000

Breach of Terms
While studying the device on [special project asteroid name] we inadvertently triggered a failsafe, resulting in an all frequencies alert siren. Despite initial predictions this would be unanswered, an automated drone has entered our territory.

Our hails all receive the same response:

"Greetings valued customer, our records show you are attempting to create an unlicensed version of the latest MKIII Georadiation Matrix, a clear violation of CoburnCorp's End User License Agreement. We will now be enforcing our legal authority to terminate your attempts with extreme prejudice. Any complaints can be directed towards our customer service drones."
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Undetonated Device
 Is triggered only by:

Device Analysis special project has 25% progress

Open communications.
  • −3000
We will not negotiate with "customer support".
  • A fleet of 4 hostile drones enter the system

Device Destroyed
We have successfully dismantled the device on [special project asteroid name], salvaging any materials that could be repurposed for our benefit.

With this device destroyed and its predecessors in ruin, the secrets of this destructive technology are lost to the galaxy forever.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Undetonated Device
  • Did not issue the Device Analysis special project
 Mean time to happen:

30 days

Good riddance.
  • Dark Matter Gained: 150-2000
  • Rare Crystals Gained: 150-2000
  • Alloys Gained: 150-2000
  • Engineering Research Gained: 350-100000
  • Society Research Gained: 350-100000

Terms Modified
With the customer service drones dealt with, we are now able to resume retro-engineering the Georadiation Matrix.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Breach of Terms
 Is triggered only by:

Paid Energy or destroyed the attacking fleet

Continue studying the device.
  • Alloys Gained: 100-1000

Undetonated Device
After extensive study, we have successfully retro-engineered the MKIII Georadiation Matrix, unlocking its potential with added improvements to the process.

By integrating radioactive molecular destabilization into our terraforming equipment, we can stimulate the growth of valuable resources.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Terms Modified
 Is triggered only by:

Finishing the second Device Analysis special project

Excellent news.
  • Engineering Research Gained: 250-100000
  • Society Research Gained: 250-100000
  • Gain the Georadiation Terraforming technology
