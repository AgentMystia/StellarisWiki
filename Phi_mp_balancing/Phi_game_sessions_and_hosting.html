Rules

Standard rules

  1. Moderators rule. Their decisions are final.
  2. Everyone shall behave or face sanctions.
  3. No memes empire and races. respect the roleplay side of the game.
  4. All players shall act in accordance of their empires' ethics, civics and government form.
  5. Diplomatic acts shall be ratified in game. No "voice" pacts just to save influence. No pre-game treaties.
  6. Disclosing your position or having diplomacy between players before the in-game comms are researched is forbidden.
  7. War invitations (join war) are restricted to empires bordering one of the attackers or the defending empire.
  8. No favours trading.
  9. No exploits (e.g. negative resources builds, infinite alloys, etc...)
  10. No Lost Colony origin.
  11. Restricted governments:
    • 1 megacorp max / 5 players
    • 1 gestalt max / 5 players
    • 1 homicidal max / 5 players
    • 1 homicidal max per type
Galaxy settings
