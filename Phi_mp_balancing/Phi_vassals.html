Subject types

There are a number of types of subjects available depending on the overlord's government. Most of them have to pay a certain tribute to the overlord.

PHI disables the option to integrate a vassal.

Type Tribute Can expand Diplomacy Protected Joins overlord's wars Get sensors Other effects Overlord requirements
Tributary
  • 10% Energy Output
  • 10% Minerals Output
  • Not Corporate authority
Vassal
  • 10% Alloys Output
  • 10% Consumer Goods Output
  • 10% Research Output
  • Subject gains:
    • Research agreement
    • Research sharing bonuses
  • Overlord gets:
    • −1 Monthly Influence
  • Not Corporate authority
  • Less than 50% technological superiority
  • Neither empire has Inward Perfection civic
Protectorate
  • Subject gains:
    • −80% Cost on overlord's tech
  • Overlord gains:
    • +1 Monthly Influence
    • +200 Monthly Society Research
  • At less than 50% Technological Level difference the Protectorate becomes a Vassal
  • Not Corporate authority
  • More than 50% technological superiority
  • Neither empire has Inward Perfection civic
Subsidiary
  • 5% Energy Output
  • 5% Minerals Output
  • 5% Alloys Output
  • 5% Consumer Goods Output
  • 5% Research Output
  • Subject gains:
    • Research agreement
    • Research sharing bonuses
  • Overlord gets:
    • −1 Monthly Influence
  • Corporate authority
