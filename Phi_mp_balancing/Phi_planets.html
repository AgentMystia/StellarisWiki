Admin Cap

Players have 400 Administrative Capacity points at game start.

Void Dwellers get a bonus +50 Administrative Capacity.

Declaring a world a Resort Colony will grant a bonus +75 Administrative Capacity.

Empire sprawl

Empire Sprawl is a measure of an empire's expansion. Empire Sprawl is increased according to the following table.

Source Empire Sprawl
District +1
System +2
Colony +100
Branch Office +5
Pop +0.75

Regardless of being above or below the Admin Capacity, an empire will get the following modifiers based on its total sprawl:

  • +1.00% Campaign (Subset of Edicts) cost
  • +0.10% Leader upkeep
  • +0.10% Leader recruitment cost

Penalties

Each point the Empire Sprawl exceeds the Administrative Capacity will impart the following penalties:

  • +1.00% Tech cost
  • +1.00% Tradition adoption cost
  • −0.50% Government Ethics attraction
  • +0.50 Crime
  • +1.00% Leader upkeep

Planet Modifiers

When firstly explored, each habitable standard planet will get a randomly choosen modifier. The "planet type" linked to each modifier represent the planet class most suitable for that modifier to appear in (probability is never zero). On the planet interface, under the Decision tab, a player can always find a special decision to improve these modifiers if the necessary technology has been discovered.
