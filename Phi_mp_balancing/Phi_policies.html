Diplomatic Stance

Cannot be changed while the TORA! TORA! TORA! modifier is active (it is applied to any empire entering a war and lasts 1 year).

TORA! TORA! TORA!
  • −10% Diplomatic Weight
  • Cannot change military stance
We are suddenly at war, and changing diplomatic posture will require some time.

Normal empires

Stance Effects Requirements Description
Disarmament
  • +1 merchant job per 20 pops
  • +1 clerk job per 10 pops
  • −20% Ship Upkeep
  • +20% Diplomatic weight from Economy
  • +10% War exhaustion gain
  • −20% Ship fire rate
  • −20% Diplomatic weight from Fleet Power

Let's prosper in peace.

  • +1 clerk job per 20 pops
  • −10% Ship Upkeep
  • +10% Diplomatic weight from Economy
  • +5% War exhaustion gain
  • −10% Ship fire rate
  • −10% Diplomatic weight from Fleet Power
  • Any must be true:
    • has Disarmament stance
    • has Neutral stance

Cut the military budget and invest those resources for our people.

Neutral
  • Any must be true:
    • has Disarmament stance
    • has Conscription stance

We must stand ready for everything.

Conscription
  • +1 soldier job per 20 pops
  • −5% War exhaustion gain
  • +10% Ship fire rate
  • +10% Diplomatic weight from Fleet Power
  • +10% Ship Upkeep
  • −10% Diplomatic weight from Economy
  • Any must be true:
    • has Neutral stance
    • has Full Scale Mobilization stance

Enlist our youngs and keep the military machine well oiled.

Full Scale Mobilization
  • +1 noble job per 20 pops
  • +1 soldier job per 10 pops
  • −10% War exhaustion gain
  • +20% Ship fire rate
  • +20% Diplomatic weight from Fleet Power
  • +20% Ship Upkeep
  • −20% Diplomatic weight from Economy
  • All must be true:
    • has completed Supremacy
    • has Conscription stance

Total war is now inevitable. Throw everything and everyone in!

Genocidal Empires

Cannot be changed while the TORA! TORA! TORA! modifier is active. The one reported below applies to Fanatic Purifiers, but apart from a name and flavour change, the bonuses/maluses are the same also for Determined Exterminators and Devouring Swarms.

Stance Effects Requirements Description
Blood Orgies

+10% Pop Growth

−10% Ship speed

  • has Purification stance
 Our warriors mate and grow over xenos bloodied corpses.
Purification
  • Any must be true:
    • has Blood Orgies stance
    • has Exhaltation stance
Exhaltation

−10% Pop Growth

+10% Ship speed

  • has Purification stance
 Our scouts saw you. We now come and purify.
