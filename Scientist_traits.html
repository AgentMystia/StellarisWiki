Most traits have multiple tiers and can be upgraded. The way in which leaders gain or upgrade traits depends on whether the Galactic Paragons DLC is enabled:

Veteran traits

With the Galactic Paragons DLC, leaders that reach skill level 4 can select a veteran class, which will unlock a pool of veteran traits selectable on the next level-ups. Without the Galactic Paragons DLC, leaders do not use veteran classes, but can randomly gain some of these traits at level-up if their requirements are met.

Expertise traits

Expertise traits increase the research speed of a certain research field when assigned to the council. Each scientist can only gain one expertise trait from each research area when leveling up, but more expertise traits from the same area can be added by events. They can be selected if the leader has the Statistician veteran class. If the empire has the Technocracy civic, each scientist starts with a random tier I expertise trait, and the traits gain additional effects when governing a planet or sector.

Without the Galactic Paragons DLC, they can be gained randomly upon level-up if their requirements are met.
