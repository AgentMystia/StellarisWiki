Caravan events can take place while a caravan is present within an empire's borders after first contact has been completed with the Caravaneers. Once a caravan event takes place the same empire will not receive another caravan event for 2 years and will not receive the same event for 10 years.

Caravaneers: Old with Defects

Our starbase in the [system name] system reports that the [caravan name] fleet has evacuated and abandoned a decrepit ship, now in unstable orbit around the system's central star.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • None
 Mean time to happen:

24 months

One man's trash...
  • Issue Special Project: Search Abandoned Caravaneer Ship
Tow it to the starbase for disposal.
  • Consumer Goods Gained: 150-1000
Let the sun swallow it.

Caravaneers: Thieves!

After a recent visit of the [caravan name] to [colony name] it has been noticed that a number of resources are missing from the central reserve. It is suspected that the [caravan name] somehow made off with them.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • System has a colony
 Mean time to happen:

12 months

My precious!
  • Energy Credits: −200

Caravaneers: Local Franchise

An urgent missive from [colony name] explains that a number of now-former members of the [caravan name] seek political asylum on the planet. The local administration has seen fit to pass the decision of what to do with them up the chain of command.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Not Gestalt Consciousness
  • System has a colony with at least 200 Pops
 Mean time to happen:

24 months

All may contribute to the [empire name].
  • The colony gains 300 free Pops
Turn them away.
  • Influence: +30 if Xenophobe or Fanatic Xenophobe

Caravaneers: Greener Grass

Reports from [colony name] inform us that a number of inhabitants have left the colony to join the [caravan name] fleet passing through our territory, ostensibly drawn by dreams of adventure or the heady aroma of raw mercantilism.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Not Gestalt Consciousness
  • System has a colony with at least 600 free Pops
 Mean time to happen:

24 months

Unfortunate.
  • The colony loses 300 free Pops
This is an outrage!
  • The colony loses 300 free Pops
  • Influence: −10

Caravaneers: A warm welcome!

As the [caravan name] entered the orbit of [colony name] for what should have been a brief courtesy visit today, a spontaneous outbreak of goodwill turned into a combination of open-air market and party. Many bonds between [founder species plural] and [caravan species plural] were forged, and much trading took place.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Xenophile, Fanatic Xenophile, Materialist or Fanatic Materialist
  • System has a colony
 Mean time to happen:

12 months

Come back soon!
  • Energy Credits Gained: 150-1000
  • 33% chance for the world to gain 300 pops from the caravaneer species a year later

Caravaneers: Begone!

Mass protests erupted on [colony name] today, due to outrage over the [caravan name] in the system. Their grubby presence offends all right-thinking [founder species plural].
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Xenophobe or Fanatic Xenophobe
  • System has a colony
 Mean time to happen:

12 months

Order them to avoid [colony name]
  • Influence Gained: 80-175
