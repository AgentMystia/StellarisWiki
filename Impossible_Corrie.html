Impossible Corrie is a colony event chain that has a very small chance to trigger 2 or 3 years after any colony has been founded. It can only happen once per game and cannot happen to empires with the Wilderness origin.

Impossible Corrie

A geological anomaly has come to our attention on [colony name]. We began surveying after a bowl-shaped depression suddenly appeared alongside a mining facility there. Features like this are almost never found outside of a glacial region, but stranger still: the walls of this 'cirque' scatter visible light in a flower-petal pattern, and have been found to resonate when exposed to certain electro-magnetic bands. Investigations are ongoing.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Not capital world
  • Colony is not a Ringworld or Habitat
  • Colony has Miner jobs
 Mean time to happen:

30 months

Curious.
  • Adds the Lotus Corrie planetary feature to the colony

Project Blue Lotus

Our scientists report that the newly-uncovered, crystalline blue deposits on [colony name] respond dramatically to delayed-erasure photon energy. The minerals filter its wavelengths and reflect a beam which is capable of transforming ordinary silicates into a new, super-conductive alloy — though only ever during daylight.

Given that this process essentially involves bouncing high levels of radiation around an enclosed space, some scientists have already come out in protest. A different group of excited researchers offers a counter-argument, stressing that this phenomenon could revolutionize the way we refine precious metals. Either way: developing this technique further will be costly.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Impossible Corrie
 Mean time to happen:

45 days

Overly-Keen Proponents

Regrettable scenes on [colony name], as a group of obsessive scientists sought to intervene in the project's closure. Dazzled by their own research, they attempted to wrest control of the transmutation experiments 'for the benefit of new science.' The resulting scuffle caused equipment to be damaged, and a runaway cascade took hold.

What remains of the cirque is something akin to a geode: a rocky shell around what used to be the equipment and protestors, now re-rendered in crystalline form.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Project Blue Lotus
  • Not Gestalt Consciousness
  • Colony does not have the Blue Lotus Prototype planetary feature
 Mean time to happen:

40 days

Unfortunate.
  • Alloys Gained: 100-500

Alchemy in Motion

The laboratory on [colony name] is yielding verifiable results. Although their process can still only transmute small amounts of rock into alloys, researchers have high hopes for a larger-scale operation. They have requested access to further resources.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Project Blue Lotus
  • Colony has the Blue Lotus Prototype planetary feature
 Mean time to happen:

55 days

OK

Lotus Collapse

The transmutation experiments at [colony name] have failed - though their failure may actually have prevented future catastrophe. When new, more refined equipment was first brought online, it ran well within design tolerances. However, when shut-down instructions were issued, the process accelerated beyond the reach of its failsafes. Conversion took hold in the facility's housing, transmuting it and its surroundings into the same, super-conductive alloy.

The resulting ground explosion has taken a number of lives along with significant chunks of the surrounding landscape. We're left with little other than a seam of good material, and a cautionary tale in metaphysics.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Alchemy in Motion
 Is triggered only by:

The Blue Lotus Facility special project expires

Let's not try that again.
  • Adds the Blue Lotus Ruin planetary feature to the colony
  • 100 pops die

Lotus Eats Itself

A catastrophic failure has occurred at the experimental facility on [colony name]. Operatives say we may never know what specifically caused the failure in containment, but the result was runaway transmutation of the equipment, its housing, and a vast chunk of the surrounding landscape - all into the same, superconductive alloy. A rich seam of this material is all that remains.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Colony has the Blue Lotus Facility planetary feature
 Mean time to happen:

320 days

Unfortunate.
  • Removes the Blue Lotus Facility planetary feature from the colony
  • Removes the Lotus Corrie planetary feature from the colony
  • Adds the Blue Lotus Crater planetary feature to the colony
  • All Pops with Metallurgist jobs are killed
