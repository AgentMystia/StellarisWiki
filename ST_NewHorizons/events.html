Story Events (United Earth)

United Earth is one of the major powers in ST: New Horizons. There are only two events specifically dedicated to United Earth, the rest of the events are dedicated to the United Federation of Planets.

Terra Prime Crisis (Demons)

Terra Prime is a xenophobic isolationist extremist group from Star Trek: Enterprise, with the intent of removing all non-humans from Earth and the Sol System. [1] They were not opposed to the usage of violence and threatening to achieve their goals, and during the show, they had hijacked a terraforming array and attempted to destroy San Francisco with it, however, they were stopped by the crew of the NX-01 Enterprise.

In-game, the player is faced with the same situation as United Earth faced in the show, and the player must make decisions in regards to how they will deal with this wave of xenophobia, and whether they will reject it, or embrace it. Main article: Terra Prime Events

Xindi Crisis

The Xindi Crisis is an event chain that occurs when the player is playing as United Earth. With an MTTH at 60 months (Approx. 5 Years), it will trigger in 2155 or later, about when it happened in Star Trek: Enterprise (Spoilers). Main article: Xindi Crisis Events

Terra Nova

For those of you who aren't Trekkies (I would highly suggest starting), Terra Nova was the first attempt at a colony by Earth in a century, and it failed miserably. PS: Huge spoiler alert for ST: Enterprise, don't read if you plan on watching.

United Federation Of Planets

"A dream that became a reality and spread throughout the stars." – James T. Kirk,

The United Federation of Planets (abbreviated as UFP and commonly referred to as the Federation) was an interstellar federal republic, composed of planetary governments that agreed to exist semi-autonomously under a single central government based on the principles of universal liberty, rights, and equality, and to share their knowledge and resources in peaceful cooperation, scientific development, space exploration and defensive purposes.

One of the most powerful interstellar states in known space, it encompassed 8.000 light years. The total number of formal member worlds was over one hundred and fifty.

Unlike its imperial rivals, who derived power from a single species subjugating other races, the Federation's various member worlds joined willingly and were equals in the Federation's democratic society. The Federation Starfleet was incorporated to maintain exploratory, scientific, diplomatic, and defense functions.

In the alternate reality, Captain Christopher Pike described the Starfleet, the Federations primary space-borne navy, as a "peacekeeping and humanitarian armada."

How to create United Federation of Planets
  • 1) You must choose one of United Earth, Vulcan, Andorian or Tellarite as your starting race.
  • 2) You must NOT be currently in the middle of a "crisis" (e.g. Xindi or Demons).
  • 3) You must be in a Coalition with three other Coalition members.
  • 4) At least two of these Coalition members need to be from the original four founding races (Human, Vulcan, Andorian or Tellarite).
  • 5) At least 15 years must have passed
  • Console Troubleshoot Reference: "STNC_federation_mechanics.1001"
