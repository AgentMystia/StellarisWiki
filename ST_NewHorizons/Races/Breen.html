Breen Confederacy

"Never turn your back on a Breen"- A Romulan saying

Shrouded in mystery, the Breen are a species of seeming humanoids native to the planet Breen. The Breen constantly wear refrigeration suits as their physiology is among the most exotic for humanoid lifeforms, which might have been due to the climate of their home planet. Given that most known humanoid species lived on class M planets with an average temperature far above the supposed temperature of Breen, the Breen were largely thought to have had to develop refrigeration suits in order to co-exist with others. The Breen are known to use slave labour of lesser races in their mines and construction programs.
