Strange Station

The space station on the other side of the astral rift

Strange Station is a unique rift that can potentially create an inaccessible empire to trade with. It cannot spawn for empires with the Riftworld origin and has the following chapters:

  1. small astral threads reward (40, 50, or 60).
  2. small astral threads reward (40, 50, or 60) and a moderate amount of Engineering or Society if not Gestalt Consciousness and Unity if Gestalt Consciousness.
  3. small astral threads reward (40, 50, or 60) and a choice between an easier and a harder choice.
  4. If chose the harder choice, gain a large amount of Society and +50% Governing Ethics Attraction for 10 years. If Gestalt Consciousness, the empire gains the Fractured Ambassadors permanent modifier. In addition, establish communications with the Mirror Empire. If chose the easier choice, gain a very large amount of Unity and medium astral threads reward (90, 100, or 110).

If the more difficult path was chosen, then the Mirror Empire is added to the contacts menu. The Mirror Empire has the same species but an opposed ethic, the fanatic one if the empire has a fanatic ethic. It always has positive relations due to a +150 Opinion bonus. The only possible interactions are trading resources, and either forming a Commercial Pact or Rivalry (which does not have the usual restrictions). The Mirror Empire has unlimited resources to trade but always refuses if it's Xenophobe or Fanatic Xenophobe.

Available only with the Grand Archive DLC enabled.

Finishing the astral rift grants the Interdimensional Treaty specimen
