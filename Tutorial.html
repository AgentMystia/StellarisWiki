Tutorial

The Tutorial is a series of voicelines and textboxes provided for the player when beginning a new campaign. They consist of the default advisor voice, a helpful robot NPC named VIR, introducing game concepts to the player and informing them of the core gameloop of Stellaris as the player explores the user interface.

The tutorial can be set between Full Tutorial, which plays all voicelines and provides unique projects in the situation log to ensure the player can explore and expand correctly, Tips, which only plays the voicelines required to inform the player but does not provide any additional guidance, and No Tutorial, which disables all voicelines and projects. the tutorial settings can be changed either in the gamplay section of the settings menu or in VIR's opening dialogue. Playing Gestalt Consciousness disables the tutorial regardless of settings.
