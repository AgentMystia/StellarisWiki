Imminent Eruption is a colony event chain that has a very small chance to trigger 1 year after any colony has been founded in a Volcanic World. It can only happen once per game.

Initial event

Imminent Eruption
Geological alert systems warn that a volcanic eruption near the main settlement on [colony name] is likely to occur within the next year.

Investing in a controlled pressure release could allow the lava flow to be harnessed for energy production. Crews could also be put on standby to harvest magma directly from the eruption and strain it for precious metals. Alternatively, we could focus on evacuating civilians and supplies from the area to minimize risk.

We can also continue to monitor the situation before committing to any action, in case the predictions are inaccurate.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Volcanic World
 Mean time to happen:

1 year

Evacuate!
  • −500
Wait and see.
Focus on power generation.
Harvest the magma.

Outcomes

Depending on the selected option, one of the following outcomes will happen:

Just in time
The early evacuation of the colony turned out to be a good choice. The volcano erupted sooner than expected, severely damaging the buildings in the area. Now that the underground volcanic pressure is minimal, the colonists can return and safely rebuild.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Chose Evacuate!
 Mean time to happen:

5 days

Excellent.

Enhanced Power-Grid
Engineering crews were able to establish a safe system for slowly releasing pressurized lava from the underground chambers, passing it through power generators as it went. This has resulted in a short-term boon to the colony's electrical grid.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Chose Focus on power generation.
 Mean time to happen:

5 days

Excellent.
  • Energy Gained: 250-5000

Magma Harvesting
The volcanic eruption was interrupted by a combination of ultrasonic disruptors and physical barriers, resulting in a large field of magma contained and hardening on the surface. Mining crews have already begun harvesting this mineral bounty.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Chose Harvest the magma.
 Mean time to happen:

5 days

Excellent.
  • Minerals Gained: 350-10000

Volcanic Wrath
The volcano exploded early, and the colony was not prepared to deal with the catastrophe. Massive damage occurred to infrastructure, and loss of life was significant.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Chose Wait and see.
  • 50% chance
 Mean time to happen:

5 days

Unfortunate.
  • 200 Pops die

Wait and See
After a few months, volcanic activity near the [colony name] colony has subsided again. While the long-term safety risk has not disappeared, it appears the colony is safe for the time being.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Chose Wait and see.
  • 50% chance
 Mean time to happen:

5 days

The risks paid off.
  • Unity Gained: 150-100000
