The Mirror Empire

Upon establishing contact with the Mirror Empire they are added to the player's contacts menu, where they will be for the rest of the game. There is no way to remove the Mirror Empire.

The Mirror Empire has the same leader species but has opposite ethics. For example, if the player empire has Authoritarian and Fanatic Spiritualist then the Mirror Empire will have Egalitarian and Fanatic Materialist. If the player empire is a Gestalt Consciousness then the Mirror Empire will be also.

Relations with the Mirror Empire are always positive due to a permanent +150 Opinion bonus. The only possible interactions are forming a Commercial Pact, forming a Rivalry (which does not have the usual restrictions), or trading resources. The Mirror Empire has unlimited resources to trade. If the Mirror Empire is any degree of File:Xenophobe ethic gradient.png Xenophobe then it will always refuse trade offers.
