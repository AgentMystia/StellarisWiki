Astral threads
The astral actions tab

Astral threads are filaments of condensed space-time and the result of dimensional planes colliding with one another. They can only be found on Astral Scar celestial objects or by completing astral rifts.

Empires can initially store up to 500 astral threads. Each completed astral rift increases the storage capacity by 250 and each Astral Siphon building increases it by 100 per tier.

Astral actions

Astral threads are mainly used for astral actions, which give the empire an instant benefit. Most astral actions requires the completion of a number of astral rifts before they are unlocked. Actions are either limited-use which typically unlock a mechanic permanently, or repeatable with a single-use or timed benefit per activation of the action. A number of actions increase in cost with each use, up to a maximum cost.
