Stasis Prison is a colony event chain that has a very small chance to trigger 5 years after any colony has been founded on a planet that has a moon. It can only happen once per game.

Contents

  • 1 Missed The Morning Check-in
  • 2 Lucky Scan
  • 3 Holding Pattern
  • 4 Escape From The Moon
  • 5 Bad Neighbors
  • 6 Mixed Bag
  • 7 Good Neighbors

Missed The Morning Check-in
Recently, numerous [colonists or drones] have gone missing on [colony name]. The only discernible pattern is that each was alone at the time of their disappearance. As news began to spread, a collective fear of going unescorted has gripped the colony. This state of unrest is causing considerable problems for our day-to-day operations.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Not capital world
  • Colony has a moon
  • Colony has at least 400 pops
 Mean time to happen:

60 months

Implement tracking procedures.
  • −100
  • −5000
  • Thankful Tracking modifier added, giving the following effects:
    • Upkeep from Jobs +10%
    • Stability +10
  • Next event happens 6 months earlier
There is no need to panic.
  • −100
  • Collective Autophobia modifier added, giving the following effects:
    • Upkeep from Jobs +10%
    • Stability −10

Lucky Scan
We've had a lucky break on [colony name]! We have uncovered the location of the most recent [colonists or drones] to go missing. A detailed scan of the area revealed a decaying residue of charged particles.

We've established a special project to determine the true nature of the particles and their involvement in our missing [colonists or drones].
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Missed the Morning Check-in
 Mean time to happen:

12 months

Get to the bottom of this.
  • Issue Special Project: Particle Mystery

Holding Pattern
Using the residue as a reference, a system-wide scan found evidence of the particles within [colony name]'s moon.

A search of the lunar surface revealed a few small airlocks buried under centuries of dust. Inside was an abandoned complex of admin offices, long-range matter transporters, and field manipulation systems connected to a network of highly advanced stasis pods.

Frozen in the pods were many individuals, including our missing colonists. There is little doubt that this facility functioned as a prison. We have yet to determine why they were taken and how we can free them.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Lucky Scan
 Is triggered only by:

Finishing the Particle Mystery special project

Excellent.

Escape From The Moon
Far from a conspiracy, our missing [colonists or drones] were imprisoned because of a glitch.

A computer error reactivated the prison's aging transporter system and randomly identified our [founder species plural] as guilty. The facility's architects used the pods for both prisoner storage and re-education. Freeing our people was relatively easy, but many original prisoners are still stuck in stasis fields. It should be possible to release them, although the lack of prison records means we cannot know the full extent of their criminal pasts.

Alternatively, we could restore the prison, giving special wardens control of its transporter system.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Holding Pattern
 Mean time to happen:

60 days

A useful device for wayward [colonists or drones].

Bad Neighbors
Most of the released prisoners on [colony name] were hardened criminals. Having the group of them suddenly released has been causing issues for the colony.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Escape From The Moon
  • Did not gain any planetary feature
  • 25% chance if not Xenophobe or Xenophile
  • 40% chance if Xenophobe
  • 10% chance if Xenophile
 Mean time to happen:

60 days

Unfortunate.
  • Gain 400 additional pops with the Communal and Unruly traits
  • Freed Stasis Criminals modifier added for 60 months, giving the following effects: Criminal Jobs +2

Mixed Bag
The prisoners released on [colony name] were a mix of hardened criminals and political dissidents: artists, writers, and musicians.

While the cultural prisoners are happy to share their knowledge with us, lacking any other option, many of the criminals have reverted to their old ways.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Escape From The Moon
  • Did not gain any planetary feature
  • 50% chance
 Mean time to happen:

60 days

Acknowledged.
  • Gain 400 additional pops with the Communal and Unruly traits
  • Freed Stasis Criminals modifier added for 60 months, giving the following effects: Criminal Jobs +2
  • Freed Stasis Dissidents modifier added, giving the following effects: Culture Worker Jobs +2

Good Neighbors
Almost all of the prisoners released on [colony name] were political dissidents: artists, writers, and musicians.

They're happily sharing their culture and knowledge with us now that they have been given their freedom.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Escape From The Moon
  • Did not gain any planetary feature
  • 25% chance if not Xenophobe or Xenophile
  • 10% chance if Xenophobe
  • 40% chance if Xenophile
 Mean time to happen:

60 days

Excellent.
  • Gain 400 additional pops with the Communal and Unruly traits
  • Freed Stasis Dissidents modifier added, giving the following effects: Culture Worker Jobs +2
