Follow the Mysterious Chart is an event chain associated with the Treasure Hunters origin. It follows the empire's journey to decipher an ancient orbital capsule, locate coordinates across the galaxy, deal with the rival pirate faction "Black Needle", and ultimately uncover the grand hoard of Captain Ness.

Part 1: Deciphering the Capsule

The event chain begins immediately at game start.

Mysterious orbital capsule
An ancient orbital capsule has been detected orbiting our homeworld. It appears to contain part of a coordinate system and a locked database.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Has origin: Treasure Hunters
 Is triggered only by:
  • Game start
Begin deciphering.
  • Receive the Mysterious Orbital Capsule relic.
  • Spawns the Decipher Mysterious Capsule special project on the homeworld capital (costs 500 Physics research).

Deciphering completed
The capsule has been successfully deciphered, revealing coordinates to another system.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • None
 Is triggered only by:
  • Completion of the Decipher Mysterious Capsule special project.
Fascinating.
  • Mysterious Orbital Capsule relic is upgraded to Celestial Chart (Stage 1).
  • Hyperlane Breach Points technology is unlocked as a permanent research option (gives +25% progress if already researching).
  • Sublight Speed +25% for 2 years.
  • Reveals the first unique Treasure Hunters system.

Part 2: First System and Captain Hoo

Upon entering the first revealed system, a pirate fleet belonging to the "Black Needle" faction appears.

The Black Needle
The pirate fleet in this system is led by Captain Hoo. They claim this system and its secrets belong to them.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • None
 Is triggered only by:
  • Entered the first unique system.
Open communications.
  • Triggers contact event with Captain Hoo. Allows two approaches: combat or hacking.

Hacking Approach

Pirate Base Abandoned
The Black Needle have finally realized that they were duped, discovering the holes in their closed security network. With these vulnerabilities exposed, the pirates decided to abandon their base...
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Hack Black Needle Network special project completed
 Is triggered only by:

{{{triggered only}}}

A logical decision.
  • The moon becomes immediately colonizable (armies leave the planet).
  • Councilor/Leader gains +800 XP.
  • Spawns the Decode Third Map Piece special project on the capital.

Combat Approach

Captain Hoo defeated
Captain Hoo's fleet has been disabled. In exchange for their lives, they surrender their database.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Combat victory over Captain Hoo's fleet
 Is triggered only by:

{{{triggered only}}}

Secure the database.
  • Unity +moderate.
  • Reveals a Shattered Ytterite World.
  • Spawns the Recover Second Chart Piece special project on the Shattered Ytterite World.

Second Piece Recovered
The second chart piece has been successfully extracted, updating the Celestial Chart.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Recover Second Chart Piece special project completed
 Is triggered only by:

{{{triggered only}}}

Excellent.
  • Celestial Chart is upgraded to Stage 2.
  • Reveals the second unique system containing the Black Needle Base.

Part 3: The Black Needle Base

Upon surveying the Black Needle Base planet, the player must choose an assault method: orbitally bombard, storm with armies, or hack.

Stronghold in Sight
The planetary base is heavily fortified. We must choose our assault method.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • None
 Is triggered only by:
  • Surveyed the Black Needle Base planet.
Bombard them from orbit.
  • Enables the Bombard Stronghold special project.
Deploy ground forces.
  • Enables the Storm Stronghold special project.
Hack their database remotely.
  • Enables the Hack Stronghold special project.

Hacking Path

Pirate Base Abandoned
With these vulnerabilities exposed, the pirates decided to abandon their base, evacuating immediately and securing the other valuables that were stored there.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Hack Stronghold special project completed
 Is triggered only by:

{{{triggered only}}}

A logical decision.
  • The moon becomes immediately colonizable (armies leave the planet).
  • Sets the black_needle_base_stolen_tech flag.
  • Spawns the Decode Third Map Piece special project.

Assault/Bombardment Path

Stronghold Ransacked
Having breached or orbital-bombarded the stronghold, we claim the treasures inside.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Bombard Stronghold or Storm Stronghold special project completed
 Is triggered only by:

{{{triggered only}}}

Repatriate stolen items.
  • Unity +moderate.
  • Opinion +50 with all default empires.
  • Grants Black Needle Ceremonial Sword specimen.
  • Spawns the Decode Third Map Piece special project.
Sell stolen items.
  • Energy Credits +large.
  • Grants the Sold Stolen Items modifier ( Market Fee +10%) for 1 year.
  • Grants Black Needle Ceremonial Sword specimen.
  • Spawns the Decode Third Map Piece special project.
Keep stolen items.
  • Consumer Goods (or Alloys for gestalts) +moderate.
  • If Ancient Relics active: Minor Artifacts +moderate.
  • Grants Black Needle Ceremonial Sword specimen.
  • Spawns the Decode Third Map Piece special project.

Part 4: Smeegibb's Shelter

Completing the Decode Third Map Piece project reveals Smeegibb's system.

Coordinates Decoded
The map has been updated again, pointing to an uncharted shelter system.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • None
 Is triggered only by:
  • Completion of the Decode Third Map Piece special project.
Chart a course.
  • Celestial Chart is upgraded to Stage 3.
  • Materials research progress +25%.
  • Reveals Smeegibb's shelter system.

Hidden Settlement

Upon surveying the main planet in Smeegibb's shelter system, we trigger the discovery of a small settlement.

Hidden Settlement
We have found a small settlement with a population of less than a thousand sentient molluscoids.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • None
 Is triggered only by:
  • Surveyed Smeegibb's shelter planet.
Establish contact.
  • Triggers contact event with Smeegibb.

Smeegibb
A node of the hive mind found in the hidden system, Smeegibb has a tendency to lower their gaze when spoken to...
 
Trigger conditions:
  • None
 Is triggered only by:
  • Established contact with Smeegibb.
Ask about their quarters and safehouse.
  • Dialogue leads to Safehouse Discovered event.

Safehouse Discovered
Smeegibb has revealed the coordinates of an abandoned safehouse formerly occupied by themselves and Captain Ness.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • None
 Is triggered only by:
  • Dialogue with Smeegibb completed.
Let's start digging.
  • Spawns the Smeegibb's Shelter archaeological site on the planet.

Archaeological Site Chapters

Captain's Log
The excavated structure showed evidence of long periods of use. Inside, we recovered a log entry written by Captain Ness.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • None
 Is triggered only by:
  • Archaeological site Chapter 1 completed.
Keep looking.
  • If Ancient Relics active: Minor Artifacts +small and Energy Credits +small.
  • If non-gestalt and no Ancient Relics: Consumer Goods +small and Energy Credits +small.
  • If gestalt and no Ancient Relics: Energy Credits +moderate.

Killing Time
Digging deeper revealed a small stash of valuables. Notebook entries show Captain Ness reflecting on her partnership with Smeegibb.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • None
 Is triggered only by:
  • Archaeological site Chapter 2 completed.
Keep looking.
  • Energy Credits +moderate.
  • If Ancient Relics active: Minor Artifacts +small.

Something to Prove
Concealed within an air vent, we discovered Captain Ness' final journal entry.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • None
 Is triggered only by:
  • Archaeological site Chapter 3 completed.
Site cleared.
  • Grants the Captain Ness' Logbook specimen.
  • Triggers event prompting us to re-open communications with Smeegibb.

Smeegibb is Missing
Communications are down, and our scans show Smeegibb is no longer active at the settlement.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • None
 Is triggered only by:
  • Re-opening communications with Smeegibb after site excavation.
Investigate the coordinates.
  • Reveals POI to return to Smeegibb's shelter.

Part 5: The Ruined Gateway

Completing the final project on the capital reveals the coordinates to the hoard.

Chart Complete
The final coordinates point to a sealed pocket system accessible only via a ruined gateway.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • None
 Is triggered only by:
  • Completion of the Decipher Final Coordinates special project.
Locate the Gateway.
  • Celestial Chart is upgraded to Stage 4.
  • Spawns a unique system containing a ruined gateway and a Voidworm flock.
  • Triggers gateway discovery event in 30 days.

Head Treasure Hunter
With the Celestial Chart complete, our astronomers can coordinate future treasure hunts.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • None
 Is triggered only by:
  • Upgraded relic to Stage 4.
Establish a new office.
  • Unlocks the Head Treasure Hunter council position.
Incorporate the data.
  • Grants the passive empire modifier Prospect of Treasure:
    • Energy Credits +5 monthly per Ruler level
    • Treasure Trove Rewards +5% per Ruler level
    • job output +3% per Ruler level

X Marks the Chart
The completion of the Celestial Chart points us to the final gate system.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • None
 Is triggered only by:
  • 30 days after upgrading relic to Stage 4.
Press on.
  • Unity +moderate.

Gateway Voidworms

Worm-Riddled Gate
The map has led us to a derelict Gateway system guarded possessively by massive Voidworms.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • None
 Is triggered only by:
  • Entered the ruined gateway system.
Nothing will stand in our way.
  • Spawns the Clear Gateway Voidworms special project in the system.

Voidworm Clutch
A clutch of nymphs were found nestled within one of the Voidworm carcasses.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • None
 Is triggered only by:
  • Completed the carcass research project after defeating the Voidworms.
A remarkable find.
  • Grants an organic fleet of Voidworms.
Easy prey.
  • Food +moderate.
  • Minerals +moderate.
  • +1 Voidworm DNA specimen.

Investigate Gateway Technology
The Gateway is inactive. Investigating the structure will unlock insight into gateway technology.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • None
 Is triggered only by:
  • Voidworms cleared and player does NOT possess Gateway Activation tech.
Start research.
  • Spawns special project to research Gateway Activation technology, giving 50% tech progress.

Activate the Gateway
With the creatures dealt with and the technology understood, we can now activate the gateway.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • None
 Is triggered only by:
  • Voidworms cleared and player possesses Gateway Activation tech.
Reactivate.
  • Spawns the Activate Gateway project to unlock jump capabilities to the pocket system.

Treasure, Ho!
The Gateway has been activated, revealing the pocket system sketched in Captain Ness' logbook.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • None
 Is triggered only by:
  • Ruined Gateway restored.
Press on.
  • Grants a monthly Unity multiplier.
  • Spawns jump point of interest to pocket system.

Part 6: Ness's Hoard

Activating the gateway initiates the jump into the pocket system, linking the gateway and spawning the final pirate fleet led by Cobalt Quill.

Cobalt Quill
Cobalt Quill offers to buy our Celestial Chart and map. They promise riches and a powerful flagship.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • None
 Is triggered only by:
  • Entered the pocket system.
Accept their offer (Sell Map).
  • Sell map. Leads to Ending 1.
Refuse the offer (Fight).
  • Refuse and fight. Leads to Ending 2.

Ending 1: Map Sold

Ending 1 - Map Sold
We have sold the Celestial Chart and final map to Cobalt Quill's group.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Accepted Cobalt Quill's offer
 Is triggered only by:

{{{triggered only}}}

Secure the wealth.
  • Celestial Chart relic is removed.
  • Energy Credits storage is filled to maximum capacity.
  • Spawns the unique flagship Black Needle's Eye under player control.
  • Ends the event chain successfully.

Ending 2: Fight & Sabotage

Sabotage Initiated
Cobalt Quill is furious. They vow to tear down our empire's infrastructure and sabotage our systems.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Refused Cobalt Quill's offer
 Is triggered only by:

{{{triggered only}}}

Brace for impact.
  • Initiates the Black Needle Sabotage mid-game situation.
  • Spawns pirate fleets in border systems.
  • Triggers periodic sabotage events and traitor leader events.

The Black Needle Sabotage Situation Endings

During the situation, one of your leaders may be revealed as a traitor. Executing them increases situation progress by 20 at the cost of stability/population. The situation terminates in one of three ways:

Situation Failed

Situation Failed
The situation has run out of time while the pirate fleet is still active.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Situation timer expires
 Is triggered only by:

{{{triggered only}}}

We have suffered a major setback.
  • Grants the Collapsed Infrastructure modifier for 10 years:
    • Stability −10%
    • Resources from Jobs −10%
  • Spawns the Defeat Black Needle Flagship special project.

Situation Completed

Situation Ended
The situation has been brought to a close, though the enemy flagship still lives.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Situation progress reaches 100
 Is triggered only by:

{{{triggered only}}}

The threat is contained.
  • Grants the Resilient Infrastructure modifier:
    • Stability +5%
    • Resources from Jobs +5%
  • Weakens the remaining pirate fleet by 25%.
  • Spawns the Defeat Black Needle Flagship special project.

Flagship Defeated

Situation Aborted
The enemy flagship was defeated during the situation, bringing a swift end to the conflict.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Enemy flagship destroyed in battle
 Is triggered only by:

{{{triggered only}}}

A resounding victory.
  • Aborts the situation immediately.
  • Grants the Pirates Supreme modifier:
    • Ship Fire Rate +5%
    • Damage vs. Pirates +50%
  • Triggers fleet defeat event.

Black Needle Defeated
Cobalt Quill's flagship lies in ruin. The remnants of the Black Needle disperse.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • None
 Is triggered only by:
  • Flagship defeated
Secure the hoard coordinates.
  • Grants the Black Needle Ledger specimen.
  • If non-gestalt: gains the Black Needle Piracy modifier for 10 years.
  • Spawns the Explore Captain Ness' Hoard special project on the hoard asteroid.

Part 7: Captain Ness's Fate

Completing the Explore Captain Ness' Hoard special project triggers dialogue with Captain Ness, leading to the choice of hoard distribution.

Riches Beyond Measure
We must decide what to do with the legendary hoard.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • None
 Is triggered only by:
  • Completed the Explore Captain Ness' Hoard special project.
Claim it in our name.
  • Energy Credits +massive.
  • Exotic Gases +massive.
  • Rare Crystals +massive.
  • Volatile Motes +massive.
  • If uses Consumer Goods: Consumer Goods +massive.
  • If does not use Consumer Goods: Minerals +massive.
  • If Ancient Relics active: Minor Artifacts +large.
  • Grants the Perikan Tapico Jewel and Robe of the Golden Broker specimens.
Return traceable items to their rightful owners.
  • Energy Credits +large.
  • Exotic Gases +large.
  • Rare Crystals +large.
  • Volatile Motes +large.
  • Unity +massive.
  • Grants the Perikan Tapico Jewel specimen.
  • +1 Favor from all default empires in the galaxy.
  • Opinion +100 with all default empires.
Distribute this wealth among the entire galaxy.
  • Unity +massive.
  • +2 Favors from all default empires in the galaxy.
  • Opinion +200 with all default empires.
  • Grants the Galactic Philanthropy modifier for 50 years:
    • Influence +2 monthly
    • Diplomatic Weight +40%.

Our Patron

30 days after deciding the hoard's fate, Captain Ness requests to join or assist your empire.

Our Patron
Captain Ness offers her legendary services.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • None
 Is triggered only by:
  • 30 days after the hoard decision.
Recruit her as a pilot.
  • Recruits Captain Ness as a Level 10 Renowned/Legendary Admiral with:
    • Destiny Trait: Pirate Queen (Ship Capture Chance +10%, Ship Weapons Damage +15%)
    • Traits: subclass_commander_admiral, Aggressive, Trickster, Corsair II, Wrecker II, Destructive II, Victory or Death II
    • Immortality (age hidden, immune to negative traits)
  • If Letters of Marque: Unity +large.
  • If NOT Letters of Marque: unlocks the Pirate Raid Casus Belli.
Let her train our pilots.
  • Grants the passive empire modifier School of the Pirate Queen:
    • Ship Evasion +10%
    • Ship Weapons Damage +10%
    • Leader Experience Gain +10%
  • If Letters of Marque: Unity +large.
  • If NOT Letters of Marque: unlocks the Pirate Raid Casus Belli.
Let her train our drone-controllers.
  • Grants the passive empire modifier Mind of the Pirate Queen:
    • Ship Evasion +10%
    • Ship Weapons Damage +10%
    • Disengagement Chance +10%
Imprison her for her crimes.
  • Grants the passive empire modifier Rule of Law:
    • Stability +5
    • Crime −15
    • Loyalty +2 monthly
  • Imprisons Captain Ness. Leads to suicide event in 5 years.
Dismiss her.
  • Unity +small. Captain Ness leaves.
