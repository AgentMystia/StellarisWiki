Official

Paradox Interactive has its own official forum. The forum is divided into subforums based on games.

  • Paradox Interactive's Stellaris forum.
  • Twitch channel where developers discuss upcoming updates and content.
  • Youtube channel where developers discuss upcoming updates, content and community members can contribute with spotlights, tutorials or playthroughs.
  • Stellaris X relay official news about the game.

Developers Twitter

Various developers publish preview of future features, behind the scenes and interact with the community via Twitter.

  • Game director, Daniel Moregård

Unofficial

The unofficial community often hosts various events and multiplayer games. Members of the subbredit /r/Paradoxplaza often post content like AARs, bug reports, and strategy guides as a form of entertainment. Paradox Interactive staff also frequent the site on a regular basis.

IRC

  • Paradox IRC channel at #paradox on irc.rizon.net for discussion of all Paradox games

Reddit

  • /r/ParadoxPlaza, one of the largest third-party communities around, located on Reddit.
  • /r/Stellaris, another subreddit, this one dedicated to Stellaris, which also features content such as AARs, bug reports and strategy guides.
