Master of Stellaris/Factions

Game modification: Master of Stellaris

There are 22 Factions present in Master of Stellaris. Each has a Guiding Ethic and a Secondary Ethic that determines their goals and demands. Any pop that has the Guiding Ethic or the Secondary Ethic may join a faction. A faction's default Approval rating is 40, which is then modified by the Fulfillment of their active Demands.

Environmentalist

Environmentalist Factions put the ecology of planets and the galaxy above our own needs. They believe all animals should be protected, and planets left in their natural state; the only terraforming should be to Gaia worlds.

  • Guiding Ethic: Subjective
  • Secondary Ethic: Humble
Issue DLC Additional requirements to appear Fulfillment

Industrial

Industrialist Factions favor exploiting our natural resources to their full potential. They tend to prefer peace to preserve our infrastructre from the destructive force of war, but are always grateful for obtaining new sources of a much-needed resource.

  • Guiding Ethic: Envious
  • Secondary Ethic: Objective
Issue DLC Additional requirements to appear Fulfillment

Labor

Labor Factions believe that the workers of the galaxy need protection from exploitation. They recognized that the working class are the foundation of our economy, and deserve the respect that status grants. Any sapient granted the right to employment has the right to the same equal opportunities, regardless of birth.

  • Guiding Ethic: Cooperative
  • Secondary Ethic: Humble
Issue DLC Additional requirements to appear Fulfillment

Commerce

Commerce Factions wish to maximize profit from trade, even at the expense of others. They tend to prefer peaceful diplomacy to war, and an unregulated market.

  • Guiding Ethic: Envious
  • Secondary Ethic: Competitive

Socialist

Socialist Factions claim that our wealth is not owned by individuals or governments, but our society itself. They fight for a structured economy that gives equally to all whom live freely in our society, while protecting our wealth from those that are not of our nation.

  • Guiding Ethic: Gracious
  • Secondary Ethic: Cooperative
Issue DLC Additional requirements to appear Fulfillment

Sovereignty

Sovereignty Factions believe that if one is able to dominate another, that is their right by virtue of their ability. This applies to an individual of our society as much as it applies to our society's international politics. When those less able are allowed to group together and limit the accomplished, our collective potential is restrained.

  • Guiding Ethic: Competitive
  • Secondary Ethic: Libertarian
Issue DLC Additional requirements to appear Fulfillment
