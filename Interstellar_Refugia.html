Interstellar Refugia is an event chain that starts for empires with the Cosmic Dawn origin 4 months after the Deep Excavation situation reached the third stage. The event chain is completely linear, each event leading only to the next one.

Contents

  • 1 Interstellar Refugia
  • 2 Isolated Sleepers
  • 3 Wary Sleepers
  • 4 Sleeper Starport
  • 5 The Return

Interstellar Refugia
[random scientist leader] is reconstructing their stasis archives. Their latest findings suggest numerous minor [founder species plural] populations may be scattered across the galaxy, preserved in stasis as we once were.

The archives recorded these worlds' positions, but with entire stars born and lost since, locating them now requires complex interpolation. As proof of concept, [random scientist leader] has identified one such planet, but translating the full dataset is only beginning.

Meanwhile, a troubling thought arises: [capital system star] may not be our true home star, but merely one refuge among many. If so, where is our origin? And does it still matter?
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Reached stage 3 of the Deep Excavation situation
 Mean time to happen:

4 months

We must find our kin.
  • Creates an anomaly in a system 4-8 hyperlanes away from the empire capital and tracks it in the situation log
  • A random Scientist gains the Carefree trait
  • Creates a Scientist if none exists
This world is our home now.
  • Creates an anomaly in a system 4-8 hyperlanes away from the empire capital and tracks it in the situation log
  • Unity Gained: 150-100000

Isolated Sleepers
[science ship scientist] discovered an underground facility housing more of our kin in stasis, and successfully awakened them. Unlike us, they retain faint memories of their previous lives, and are sustained by stories of a great space empire that might one day return to rescue them.

They are cautious but recognize us as kin. We could present ourselves as their long-awaited saviors, heirs to that ancient glory. Or we could tell the truth: we are forging something new, and as eager to learn from them as we are to teach.

Additional references in these archives allowed us to narrow down a second candidate with stasis pods.

Wary Sleepers
[science ship scientist] discovered more of our kin in stasis and succeeded in waking them, but they responded with fear, demanding we leave. They neither recognized us as kin nor welcomed our presence.

After tense negotiation, they expressed a desire to leave and explore the galaxy. We could offer them a simple ship at a modest cost. Or, given our constant need for labor, we relocate them to the nearest colony. Perhaps then they'll see reason.

The archives of this facility shows the location of another world with more stasis pods.

Sleeper Starport
[science ship scientist] uncovered a smaller stasis facility, along with a hidden starport housing a nearly functional science vessel. Minor repairs will suffice.

The site also held a trove of scientific data, including preserved specimens from other species, some likely ancestral to modern sentients. It's unclear if these were taken by force or given willingly, but the vessel includes holding cells designed for sentient cargo.

We stand to gain much by studying them, perhaps there may even be more lifeforms still to be discovered out there.

 Is triggered only by:

Investigating the third anomaly

Study the specimens.

Return the specimens to their relatives.
  • Unity Gained: 250-1000000
Scrap the evidence.
  • Alloys Gained: 250-5000

The Return
The sleepers who took our vessel have returned, aboard a massive warship, no less. Their journey across the galaxy brought them into conflict with pirates, who they outwitted and ultimately overthrew.

Seizing the enemy ship sparked something in them: a taste for piracy. From there, their raids grew bolder, their targets more powerful. But even successful piracy can't last forever.

Now they've come home, seeking stability, and perhaps something greater. With their ship, experience, and spoils, they could yet prove very useful.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Wary Sleepers
  • Paid Alloys
 Mean time to happen:

24 months
