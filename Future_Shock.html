All individualist empires that completed the Cyberization situation and finished the Cybernetics tradition tree will receive the Future Shock event chain in a year. The event chain keeps track of whether the empire favored people connecting their minds or remaining disconnected. At the end of the event chain the empire will have the option to switch to a new authority set based on the choices made.

Hive Mind empires do not receive this event chain as their individualist and collectivist choices are made during the situation.
