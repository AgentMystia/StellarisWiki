Empire unique buildings

Sanctums

Sanctum buildings are unlocked by gaining sufficient Attunement with a Shroud patron. Without Shadows of the Shroud they require 20 Attunement, while with Shadows of the Shroud they require 300 Attunement and are removed if the patron is replaced by Whisperers in the Void.
