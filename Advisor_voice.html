This article is for the PC version of Stellaris only.

The Advisor is an AI character responsible for verbally communicating to the player about the events within their empire, forming a core part of the user interface. The Advisor is introduced as VIR in the base game, acting as the voice of the tutorial, however many of the DLCs introduce additional voices representing different empire types or species phenotypes.

If additional voices have been made available the player can choose between an automatically selected voice, based on an empire's species or ethics, or a custom selection from any of the available voices.
