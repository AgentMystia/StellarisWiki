Paragons

Paragons are unique leaders with special backstories, portraits and predefined traits that can be obtained as part of certain events. Most paragons have their destiny trait unlocked early and instead gain another veteran trait at level 8. All paragons have a veteran class, even if the Galactic Paragons DLC is not enabled.

AI empires can not recruit any renowned or legendary paragons.

Enclave paragons

Enclave paragons can only be obtained by hiring them from enclaves. All of them start at level 5. Unlike other paragons, their name is randomized. If the Galactic Paragons DLC is enabled, each enclave paragon unlocks a new council position only they can be assigned to.

If the enclave it was hired from turns hostile, its enclave paragon leaves the empire. If they held a council position, the current agenda loses 20% progress. If the empire unlocked the enclave paragon's unique council position, it is able to reselect council positions.

Nomadic paragons

Available only with the Nomads DLC enabled.

Nomadic paragons can be recruited from the Champion's Forge Live! enclave by Nomadic empires that have at least 10 Opinion with the enclave. All of them start at level 4. Recruiting them costs 10 Energy per Empire Size. Only one nomadic paragon can ever be recruited, even if they die or are dismissed. If a paragon's recruitment is refused they can still appear subsequent times when the enclave is asked for a paragon. Empires that have a founder species portrait that looks alike to a nomadic paragon will have an additional dialogue option upon recruitment but it's only narrative.

Patron paragons

Koborok'Ra
Available only with the Shadows of the Shroud DLC enabled.

Empires that breached the Shroud can encounter the paragon of the patron they have most Attunement with. If the empire formed a Covenant, it will not be able to encounter the paragons of other patrons. The chance to encounter a patron paragon each year is 0.01% for every point of Attunement with the major patron, or 3% in case of the End of the Cycle. All patron paragons start at level 8 and have the Psychic trait.

Astrocreator Azaryn

Astrocreator Azaryn
Available only with the Galactic Paragons DLC enabled.
Class Paragon trait Other traits Ethic Age Skill level
  • Scientist
  • Scholar
  • Terraformation Nuclei
  • Perceptive Mentor
  • Xeno-Cataloger
  • Spark of Genius
 Xenophile Immortal

Astrocreator Azaryn has a 1% chance to appear every 5 years in empires that are not Militarist or Gestalt Consciousness. Once that happens, a random empire is contacted twice. The second time, it will offer a special project that requires 180 days. Completing the project issues a second special project that requires 1000 society research. Completing the second project grants 2000 society research and issues a third special project that requires 4000 society research. Completing the third project grants 5000 society research. After each special project, the empire is asked two questions. 60 days after all projects have been completed, Astrocreator Azaryn is recruited. The empire capital gains a bonus to happiness, pop growth speed, resources from jobs, worker output, and researcher output for 2 years. How large the bonus is depends on the answers to the questions. The following answers increase the effect of the modifier:

  • Is this a trick question?
  • Yes, we would.
  • We would.
  • Yes. Decisive actions require consideration.

Azaryn commands a science ship that has the ability to terraform three planets into Gaia Worlds with the Overgrowth modifier at the cost of 10000 Energy and 10 years. Barren Worlds can be terraformed too, but the time is increased to 25 years. Ecumenopolis planets cannot be terraformed. The science ship merely starts the process and does not have to wait. Azaryn's appearance changes each time a terraforming process is started, and once the third terraforming process is started, she dies, which unlocks the Contained Ecosphere empire building and awards the Dawn Of A Million Souls achievement.

Each time a terraforming process is started, there is a 25% chance to be offered a special project. Completing the special project grants 5000 society research and the next terraforming technology. If all terraforming technologies have been researched, it instead grants an additional 15000 society research. There is a 25% chance that a few months after the special project has been completed, the empire must pay either 2500 energy or 1000 society research and 500 minerals and alloys.

Keides, Scion of Vagros

Keides, Scion of Vagros
Available only with the Galactic Paragons DLC enabled.

Keides, Scion of Vagros can be recruited after 20 years by the first empire that investigates the Dugar unique system. An event 10 years before he becomes available lets the empire choose which ethic Keides has. Gestalt Consciousness empires can find the system but cannot recruit him.

Class Paragon trait Other traits Skill level
Scientist Nebulous Past
  • Erudite
  • Scrapper

A few months after Keides is recruited, he adds 3 Points of Interest to the Situation Log. Entering those systems adds a special project in each that needs 2 years and can only be researched by him. Completing each special project upgrades the The Key relic. Once all 3 special projects are completed, the empire receives one final special project that requires 4000 physics research. Completing it grants 6000 physics research and creates a wormhole in the capital system leading to the Sursect system, where Keides can fully upgrade The Key to its final form after and excavate the Vagrosian Ruins archaeological site to gain the Ancestral Inheritance trait, as well as an option to change leader class. The A Tad Too Late achievement will be awarded as well.

Skrand Sharpbeak

Skrand Sharpbeak
Available only with the Galactic Paragons DLC enabled.

Every time a non-AI empire reactivates a gateway, there is a 20% chance to spawn a unique and powerful avian ship. This chance jumps to 50% if the empire has previously built or reactivated a gateway or progressed the Ancient Capital City archaeological site beyond the 2nd stage. For empires with the Galactic Doorstep origin, the chances are instead 33% and 67%. In 4-8 months, the empire gains a special project that requires an army. Once the project is completed, the empire gains the option to invade the ship. There is a 20% chance to take control of the ship and an 80% chance the ship becomes hostile. Empires that are not Gestalt Consciousness or Fanatic Xenophobe have the option to gain a special project that requires a construction ship instead. Completing it recruits Skrand Sharpbeak.

Seralune

Seralune
Available only with the Nomads DLC enabled.

Seralune can be recruited by Nomadic empires from the Champion's Force Live! enclave, having a 16% chance to appear instead of a renowned paragon. She starts at the age of 17.

Class Paragon trait Other traits Ethic Skill level
  • Official
  • Delegate
 Amnesiac
  • Architectural Sense
  • Reformer
  • Politician

About two years after Seralune is recruited she will add 3 random systems to the situation log. Entering the first system has a 75% chance to do nothing and a 25% chance to continue the story. The second system is guaranteed to continue the story. When the story continues the following choices will be given:

  • Explore the remaining systems. The second entered system (if the first one didn't have nothing) will give Seralune 1500 Experience. The final entered system will give Seralune 2000 Experience.
  • Skip exploring the remaining systems.

After the remaining systems are either explored or discarded a special project will be added to a random system in the galaxy. The special project requires an arkship and 4 months and when completed Seralune will replace the Amnesiac trait with Inherited Grace and Rightful Steward and the empire will receive a second special project in the same system. The second special project needs 2 years to complete and will give the empire a civilian arkship as well the technology to construct more if it hasn't been researched already.
