Corporate civics
 Available only with the MegaCorp DLC enabled.

Corporate civics represent the business principles of the leading megacorp and can only be picked by an empire with Corporate authority or the Corporate Sovereign civic. Each empire starts with up to two civics, and this limit is increased to three civics by researching the Galactic Administration technology. Civics can be changed every 20 years by reforming the government spending 10 unity per empire size. Most civics improve a branch office holding to generate additional trade and add 200 Trader jobs to the empire capital. The bonuses stack if multiple civics improve the same holding. If a civic selected at empire creation unlocks or replaces a building, that building will be added to the homeworld.

If at any point a civic's requirements are no longer met due to a change in empire ethics or authority, it becomes inactive and provides no bonus until it is replaced or its requirements are met again. Buildings from an inactive civic remain functional but new ones cannot be constructed. Council positions from an inactive civic remain functional but if the slot is unlocked later they cannot be selected. All civic buildings are removed at the beginning of the next month if their civic is removed. Leader traits granted by civics are not lost if the civic is removed. Reforming the government while a civic is inactive requires removing it unless the civic is a permanent one.

If the Galactic Paragons DLC is enabled, each civic also unlocks a council position.

If the Shadows of the Shroud DLC is enabled, most civics also give +60 Attunement with a certain Shroud patron.
