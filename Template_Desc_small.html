This documentation is transcluded from Template:Desc small/doc. (edit | history)

Used to generate small, italic, tightly compressed text, perfect for descriptions.

Example

{{desc small|Beware always those who would be despots, under the false presumption that their desires and agendas are somehow more imperative than those of their fellows. A society that does not see to the needs and rights of all of its members is not a society - it is a crime.}}

will result in:

Beware always those who would be despots, under the false presumption that their desires and agendas are somehow more imperative than those of their fellows. A society that does not see to the needs and rights of all of its members is not a society - it is a crime.
