Template:District iconbox

[view] [edit] Template documentation
This documentation is transcluded from Template:District iconbox/doc. (edit | history)

Uses Iconbox and District templates to assemble a building-specific iconbox.

Example

{{district iconbox|city|government|name=City District|desc=These dense urban centers provide large amounts of housing and office space for clerical workers.}} results in:

City District
These dense urban centers provide large amounts of housing and office space for clerical workers.
