The small universe on the other side of the astral rift

The Microverse is a semi-unique Astral rift; each empire can only encounter it once per game.

For this rift to spawn, all of the following conditions must be met:

  • Astral Harvesting must have been researched.
  • Rift Sphere must have been researched.
  • An astral scar already exists within the empire's borders.

Once these conditions are true, upon surveying a new astral scar there is an 80% chance to issue the Astral Scar Instability special project. If the project is not started within 3 years of issue then it will expire and never be offered again. Once started the project takes 60 days to complete, during which the system will gain −50% Sublight Speed and −100% Trade Value. Finishing the project will give the option to open The Microverse astral rift or seal it for a large astral threads reward (140, 150, or 160) and a large amount of Physics Research.
