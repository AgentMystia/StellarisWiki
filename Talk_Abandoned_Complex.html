Talk:Abandoned Complex

Some information is missing from this article: chapter titles and amounts of awarded research. In addition it should be verified that the chapter that might fail is chapter 2-B and not 3-B. The information on the original astral threads page, which I used to create this one, was likewise incomplete. Here is what was originally said:

Abandoned Complex

  • 1. Choice, 'Search for the caretakers', 2 or 'Open an egg', 4. Gives small astral threads reward (40, 50, or 60).
  • 2a. Search for the caretakers, choice, 'Open an egg', 4 or 'Return with the remains', 2. Gives small astral threads reward (40, 50, or 60).
  • 2b. Open an egg, choice, 'Inject the liquid into the egg', 3 or 'Euthanize the remaining creatures', 3. Gives small astral threads reward (40, 50, or 60).
  • 3a. Return with the remains, narrative, gives small astral threads reward (40, 50, or 60), ends the rift.
  • 3b. Inject the liquid into the egg, choice, 'Do not intervene', 1 or 'Inject astral thread' 5. Gives Society Research.
  • 3c. Euthanize the remaining creatures, narrative, gives large astral threads reward (140, 150, or 160) and Physics Research, ends the rift.
  • 4a. Inject astral thread, narrative, gives large astral threads reward (140, 150, or 160) and grants the ability to build Warpling Armies, ends the rift.
  • 4b. Do not intervene/Inject the liquid into the egg (failure outcome), narrative, gives Physics Research and large astral threads reward (140, 150, or 160). Ends the rift, scientist dies within a month.
