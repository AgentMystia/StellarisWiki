Nemma World is a colony event chain that has a very small chance to trigger 2 years after any colony has been founded. It can only happen once per game and cannot happen to empires with the Wilderness origin.

It begins with a sequence of three events. The chain can be ended as early as the second event, but otherwise splits into four paths following the third event; which ones are available depends on ethics and civics.

  • Breeding the Nemma increases max districts and Society research output, up to 6 times.
  • Enslaving the Nemma gives a bonus to Society research output. If the empire is not authoritarian or Gestalt Consciousness, there is a choice to switch to the breeding program or increase Authoritarian ethics attraction.
  • Mining the Nemma creates additional Miner or Nemma Mining Drone jobs.
  • Slaughtering the Nemma increases maximum mining and agriculture districts and gives a bonus to Society research output.

Unsteady Ground
Please help with verifying or updating this infobox. It was last verified for version 4.1.
Our colony on [colony name] has encountered an odd situation: we have apparently settled on the back of a gargantuan amphibian reptile, whose bony shell is formed of various exotic minerals. Most of what we thought were local islands have turned out to be these creatures; our colonists have begun to refer to them as Nemmas.

They seem harmless enough, and as long as we don't dig too deep into their shell, they should barely notice us.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Not capital or homeworld
  • Planet is Continental or Ocean
 Is triggered only by:

2 years after finishing a colony

Carry on.
  • Moving Islands modifier added, giving the following effects
    • −10% Housing
    • +15% Society research from jobs
  • If empire has the Evolutionary Predators origin the world also gains the Nemma World: Cataloguing modifier
  • Trigger "A Daring Discovery" event in 700±30 days
