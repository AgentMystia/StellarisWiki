 Available only with the Cosmic Storms DLC enabled.

Once the Mid-Game year has been reached, empires with the Storm Chasers origin that finished the Stormfall situation will be given a unique event chain. The event chain is completely linear, each event leading only to the next until the final choice.

Fury's Sake
The cosmic vibrations are off, and the usual feats just don't evoke the same excitement they used to. There's no doubt we've accomplished incredible things, but there is a pervasive sense that something is missing.

The angst is real, and the time to act is now. To start fresh, we return to the beginning: a series of challenges custom designed to invoke maximum adrenaline!
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Finished the Stormfall situation
 Is triggered only by:

Reaching the Mid-Game year

The thrills have just begun.
  • Bad Vibes modifier added, giving the following effects: Happiness −5%
  • Adds the following objectives to the situation log:
    • Defeat a fleet inside a cosmic storm
    • Explore a certain highlighted system
    • Finish an anomaly or archaeological site
    • Spend 180 days inside a cosmic storm
    • Send a storm to another empire
    • Have a leader killed or MIA inside a cosmic storm

Challenge Completed
New heights were reached and limits have been shredded! Just the thing to get the emotions firing. We have completed the trial!

Our Storm Rider reported back with nothing but exultant cheers. The cosmic storms are a whole new level of thrill!
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Fury's Sake
 Is triggered only by:

Finished any objectives

Never a dull moment!
  • Bad Vibes modifier removed

Fever Broken
We've done it!

Setting our sights on the pure pursuit of thrills, we've met every challenge. Witness what we've accomplished in the past years!

Like the most excellent pioneers of our homeworld, we have pushed ourselves beyond our limits.

Even better, due to our engagement with nature's fury, we have gained incredible insight into the meaning of our existence.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Fury's Sake
 Is triggered only by:

Finished all objectives

We are so back!

A Chaser's Creed
Our people will never stop looking for a thrill. Every day we discover incredible situations and face greater challenges, but [ruler name] thinks we can go even further:

"The core of a cosmic storm is pure potential; the true, bleeding edge. An edge sharp enough to sever the status-quo and unleash the rush of a lifetime."

The time is ripe to provoke a storm of truly epic proportions, a nexus of joyous pandemonium: The Big One. Only two minor details remain: a final study to determine exactly how to invoke the perfect storm, and the opinion of the rest of the galaxy should we choose to do so.

Bringing something like this into being is sure to stir up some resentment.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Fever Broken
 Mean time to happen:

60 months

Intriguing.
  • Issue Special Project: Entice the Big One

Dark Matter Cultivation
The unique properties of Dark Matter make it the perfect thing to stir up The Big One. We will inject this elusive material into the spatial fabric surrounding our capital system and wait for the first ripples of the storm to appear.

However, this has already attracted some unwanted attention: our neighbors have begun to protest. "Not everyone is as stoked as us to ride this particular wave," complained one of our scientists.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • A Chaser's Creed
 Is triggered only by:

Finishing the Entice the Big One special project

Time to get started.
  • Storm Frenzy modifier added
  • −50 decaying opinion with all empires
  • Dark Matter: −200
  • Skip Special Project: Exotic Enticements
We need to save up some resources.
  • Storm Frenzy modifier added
  • −50 decaying opinion with all empires
  • Issue Special Project: Exotic Enticements

Onset of the Big One
Incredible! The nascent vibrations of the coming storm are already being felt. The work of our scientists is ongoing, but we are now one step closer to summoning The Big One.

However, our neighbors are less pleased. They claim we're meddling with powers we cannot possibly comprehend, and this has already affected our relations.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Dark Matter Cultivation
 Is triggered only by:

Finishing the Exotic Enticements special project or paying Dark Matter

It is what it is.
  • −75 decaying opinion with all empires

Feeding the Big One
The data is clear: whatever we put into this storm, something similar will come out of it. Ergo, if we feed it the right type of resources, then it will take on properties - and epic proportions- of every type of storm.

The people of the galaxy continue to express concern, but the ride of the century is just waiting to be born. Let us feed it what we have!
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Onset of the Big One
 Mean time to happen:

12 months

Feed the storm!
  • Costs 100 of all resources the empire currently has technology to exploit
  • Issue Special Project: Something Off the Menu
We will need more resources.
  • Issue Special Project: Repeat: Feeding the Big One

The Ultimate Feast
We must provide the nascent storm a wide variety of nourishment if we wish to create a storm of epic proportions. This amazing feat requires a specific sampling of resources at our disposal.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Feeding the Big One
 Is triggered only by:

Finishing the Repeat: Feeding the Big One special project

Feed the storm!
  • Costs 500 Energy, 50 Alloys, 5 Volatile Motes and 1 Rare Crystals
  • Issue Special Project: Something Off the Menu
We will need more resources.
  • Issue Special Project: Repeat: Feeding the Big One

The Final Push
The excitement is palpable. The path ahead grows clear: push further, push harder, push through. Let us become one with cosmic frenzy itself! Everything is now ready for the Big One. We just need to give one small push.

Our neighbors are already beyond livid, claiming that our so-called 'Nexus Storm' will wreak havoc on the galaxy. But are we really going to let these killjoys derail a project of such epic proportions? The time to choose is now.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Feeding the Big One
 Is triggered only by:

Finishing the Something Off the Menu special project

The Big One is our ultimate destiny!
  • −125 decaying opinion with all empires
No, we are better than this!

Genesis of the Big One
Here we are - at the precipice of a new experience. The time of ultimate calamity has arrived. There is nothing our Storm Riders won't face. No challenge we will ever deny.

Let the galaxy witness!

  • Lost Opinion

40 days

Do it now.

We have learned much. Now we understand that the galaxy is not a toy to be played with, but an adventure to be experienced.

[ruler name] said it best: "In this life, there are no shortages of thrills. We'll never stop seeking the next challenge, but we must do so with respect. This is what we have chosen. This is who we are truly meant to be.
