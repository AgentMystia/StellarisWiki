Stations are small space structures that can be constructed by construction ships. As such most stations cannot be built by Nomadic empires, but logistic ships can build waystations.

Small stations cost 100 Minerals if the empire uses a mechanical shipset and 125 Food if the empire uses a biological shipset. Each small station has an upkeep of 1 Energy.

Mining stations

Mining stations can be built by settled empires on the following celestial objects to gain their resources as monthly income:

  • Energy
  • Minerals
  • Food
  • Trade
  • Alloys
  • Exotic Gases
  • Rare Crystals
  • Volatile motes
  • Living Metal
  • Minor Artifacts

Mining stations have no upkeep if they are built on a celestial object that contains Energy.

Research stations

Research stations can be built by settled empires on the following celestial objects to gain their resources as monthly income:

  • Engineering
  • Physics
  • Society
  • Dark Matter
  • Zro
  • Unity
  • Nanites
  • Astral Threads
  • Minor Artifacts

Observation posts

Observation posts can be constructed by settled empires only around the homeworld of a pre-FTL civilization. Having such a station enables diplomacy and espionage of the pre-FTL civilization and will notify the empire of any age advancements.

If the civilization is not aware of aliens, the Observation Post will be able to conduct Passive or Aggressive observation and produce resources that scale with the Infiltration level on the pre-FTL civilization. Both types of observation can trigger various events.

Any Observation Post will slowly increase the Awareness of the pre-FTL civilization. Observation Posts can cloak if the required technology has been researched, canceling this effect.
