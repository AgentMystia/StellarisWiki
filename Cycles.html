Cycles are a Shroud-based celestial alignment mechanic exclusive to empires that possess one of the Superstitious Beliefs, Fortune Enterprise, Zodiac Nexus or Pattern Finders civics. Every 13 years, the celestial alignment changes, randomly rolling a new Cycle that applies a set of positive modifiers (benefits) and negative modifiers (penalties) to the empire.

Cycle Scaling

The positive and negative effects of a Cycle scale based the Positive Cycle Effects modifier and Negative Cycle Effects modifier, respectively.

These modifiers are influenced by:

  • Chief Medium: −13% Negative Cycle Effects
  • Executive Astrologist: +13% Positive Cycle Effects

Cycle Synergy (Resonance)

A hidden synergy exists between Shroud Covenants and Superstitious Beliefs Cycles. If your empire has formed a covenant with a major Shroud Patron, rolling their matching cycle triggers Cycle Synergy, applying a 1.5x multiplier to both the positive and negative modifiers of that cycle.
