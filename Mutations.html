Mutations are components that can be added to space fauna. Each space fauna can have 3 mutations if the Controlled Mutations technology has been researched and 6 mutations if the Advanced Controlled Mutations technology has been researched. Mutations grow in strength with the growth stage of the space creature. Mutations also have a basic resources cost that depends on the cloned space fauna.

Defensive mutations

Defensive mutations increase the durability of a creature by providing extra hit points. These come in three forms: shields, armor, and hull.

Shields

Shields form the first layer of hit points of a creature. They are effective against energy weapons but vulnerable to kinetic weapons.
