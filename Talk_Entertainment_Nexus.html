Entertainment Nexus

The entertainment nexus on the other side of the astral rift

This Rift spawns after investigating the Bright Object Detected anomaly and choosing to pay Energy. It has the following chapters:

1. Come One, Come All: "Continue investigations.", gives small astral threads reward (40, 50, or 60).
2. Anticipation: "Very well.", gives small astral threads reward (40, 50, or 60).
3. The Vaulting Vulpines: "A flippant use of impressive technology...", gives small astral threads reward (40, 50, or 60).
3. The Jaws of Life: "An interesting talent.", gives small astral threads reward (40, 50, or 60).
5. The Final Act: "A rare specimen, indeed.", gives rare crystals.
6. After the Show, gives gives small astral threads reward (40, 50, or 60), choice:
  • 2 "Meet with the vulpine acrobats."
  • 3 "Meet with the cyborg maw."
  • 3 "Meet with the contortionist."
7. Meet the Acrobats / Maw / Contortionist, gives small astral threads reward (40, 50, or 60), choice:
Met with Choices
Vulpine acrobats
  • 4 "Secure a tissue sample."
  • 2 "Tip them some energy credits.", pay 1000 energy
Cyborg maw
  • 5 "Secure a tissue sample."
  • 2 "Offer a snack.", pay 1000 food
Contortionist
  • 6 "Secure a tissue sample"
  • 2 "Tip them some energy credits", pay 1000 energy
8. Final outcome, depends on prior choices:
Met with Tissue sample Tip / snack
Vulpine acrobats
  • Extra-Dimensional Genetics
  • Random Genetics technology
  • Society research
  • Micro-Wormholes
  • Wormhole Stabilization technology
  • Otherwise, 50% progress to random Particles technology
Cyborg maw
  • Unorthodox Sample Collection
  • Revolutionary Medigel modifier: (if non-gestalt biological main species)
    • Medical Worker output boosted by 50%
  • Random Farming technology (if gestalt or not biological)
  • Extensive Life Support
  • 10% progress to random Robotics technology
Contortionist
  • Biological Crystallization
  • Spontaneous Crystallization edict:
    • Converts up to 1% of minerals income into rare crystals
  • Quantum Mineralization
  • Physics research
Regardless of outcome, gives large astral threads reward (140, 150, or 160), ends the rift.
