The Paradox Wikis network are dedicated to various games published by Paradox Interactive. Their main purpose is to allow the community build a repository of game-related knowledge (aka Community Wikis), useful for both new and experienced players and for modders.

The wikis are administered by grotaclas, Kami-sama and SolSys with the help of a community volunteer team.

Paradox Wikis discord

Wiki users and editors alike can join the Paradox Wikis on Discord for the following reasons:

  • Contact the Wiki team directly for support issues.
  • Collaborate with other editors on various wiki projects.
  • Make improvement suggestions and work with others to implement them.

Wiki forum threads

All PDX wikis have dedicated forum threads on their game's sub-forum. Users can post in these threads to ask questions or collaborate with others.

These threads are monitored by the wiki moderation team so there's a high chance of them responding quickly (up to a day or two).

Contact the team

If the issue in question is private it is possible to directly contact the wiki moderation team via a PM on the forums using the links below.

Moderators
Moderator is the highest level of power afforded to volunteers. They have the ability to block users, restrict editing of specific pages, roll back edits, delete pages, restore deleted pages, etc.
Half-ops
Half-ops serve as a middle-ground between moderators and regular users. They can't block users, but they can do many of the other things moderators can.
