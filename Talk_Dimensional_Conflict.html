This is the original information from the Astral Threads page, unmodified:

Dimensional Conflict

  • 1. Choice, 'Study its composition', 2 or 'Let's get in there', 3, gives small astral threads reward (40, 50, or 60).
  • 2a. Study its composition, narrative, gives alloys and armor tech.
  • 2b. Let's get in there, narrative, gives 25% progress towards a random biology tech.
  • 3. Choice, 'Try to activate the dodecahedron', 6 or 'Strip it for parts', 2, gives medium astral threads reward (90, 100, or 110).
  • 4a. Try to activate the dodecahedron, narrative, gives the Daedalus Seal relic.
  • 4b. Strip it for parts, narrative, gives dark matter and large astral threads reward (140, 150, or 160).
