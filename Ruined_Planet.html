The ruined planet on the other side of the astral rift

Ruined Planet is a semi-unique Astral rift: it is always present in the home system of each empire with the Riftworld origin, but it cannot appear elsewhere.

Chapter Detail

This is a detailed list of all chapters in this rift. The reward below each chapter name is issued when a selection is made, regardless of which one was chosen.

Options which indirectly force or preclude any final outcomes of the rift are noted in blue.

Chapters which terminate the rift are noted in red.

Chapter Choices
1 Ruined Planet

(no chapter reward)

"Search for remaining survivors."

Proceed to chapter 2.

2 Opposing Factions

(no chapter reward)

Only available if not Genocidal:

"Visit the Black Curtain." Proceed to chapter 3, then chapter 4-A.

"Visit the Starlight Vanguard."

Proceed to chapter 3, then chapter 4-B.

3 Lost Ancestors

(no chapter reward)

"The flow of time is vastly different here..."

Proceed based on choice from chapter 2.

"We were engineered?"

Proceed based on choice from chapter 2.
