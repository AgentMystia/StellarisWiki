The new species going through history on the other side of the astral rift

Genesis is a unique Astral rift: it can only spawn once per galaxy. It cannot spawn for empires that do not have at least one colony.

In this rift, unless the player chooses to abort in the first chapter they will build a new species from a pre-sapient life on the other side of the rift. At the end of the rift, one pop of the new species is placed on a random colony within the player's empire.
