The Doorway is a colony event chain that has a very small chance to trigger 2 or 3 years after any colony has been founded. It offers either mutually exclusive paths, determined at random, or the Plight of the Beta-Universe situation.

If Overlord DLC is enabled the event can also be triggered by the Sign of the Visitor situation.

The Doorway
What can only be described as a dimensional portal has been discovered in a remote location on [colony name]. Weather prediction algorithms noticed a strange air current, which was eventually determined to be caused by the slight leakage of atmosphere into the portal. The rate of loss is far too small for it to make any difference to life on [colony name], but the very existence of this portal raises some disturbing questions, such as where does it lead to, and could something come through the portal from the other side?
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Colony is not on a Ringworld or Habitat
 Mean time to happen:

30 months

Intriguing.
  • Adds a Portal Research Area planetary feature to the colony
  • Issue special project: Probe the Dimensional Portal on [colony name]

The special project leads to a random choice of the following events:

  • “A Dimension of Suffering”
  • “High-Energy Dimension”
  • “The Null Void”
  • “Mirror Mirror”
  • “A Dimension of Static”

The chance of each is equal, except the last has half the probability of happening and can't happen if any of the following is true:

  • the empire's homeworld is artificial (habitat, ring world, shattered ring, ecumenopolis, hive world, machine world, or relic world)
  • the empire doesn't own its homeworld
  • the empire has had this outcome before
  • the empire isn't a player

The Null Void
Please help with verifying or updating this infobox. It was last verified for version 4.3.3.
The Dimensional Portal on [colony name] leads to a dimension of pure entropy. It is difficult to even get sensor readings because the very energy of our pings is rapidly dissipated. Our best theory is that this "Null Void" is a dimension where the majority of zero-point energy is negative and so any positive energy which was once present has long since been used up. Knowing this, it might be possible to construct a weapon which can use the Null Void's energy destruction offensively.
