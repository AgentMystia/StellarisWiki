Junkyard Orbit is a colony event chain that has a very small chance to trigger 4 or 5 years after any colony has been founded. It can only happen once per game and cannot happen to empires with the Wilderness origin.

Contents

  • 1 Junkyard Orbit
  • 2 Veiled in Debris
  • 3 Orbital Clean-Up Complete
  • 4 Clear Skies
  • 5 Ground Locked
  • 6 Shooting Stars

Junkyard Orbit
The latest report from [colony name] informs us that we have miscalculated the planet's waste management capabilities. Massive dumpsites have merged to form a single, impassable wasteland, one that stretches to the very edge of the planet's population centers.

The locals have begun to deploy large shipments of garbage into orbit, but only as a 'temporary measure.'
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Original owner
  • Not capital world
  • Not Wilderness
  • Colony is not a Ringworld, Ecumenopolis or Habitat
 Mean time to happen:

54 months

Alarming.
  • Adds a Spreading Landfill blocker to the colony

Veiled in Debris
There has been an accident.

Due to a critical error, several trash pods orbiting [colony name] collided, causing large amounts of debris to enter the lower atmosphere. This has already limited the planet's spacefaring capabilities, and according to estimates, the situation could further worsen as the space trash continues to break down. However, launching an operation to collect the debris might prove long and costly.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Junkyard Orbit
 Mean time to happen:

12 months

Space is big. A little garbage is no concern.
  • Trash Rings modifier added
Clean the orbit.
  • Trash Rings modifier added
  • Adds an Astrogarbage Unit planetary feature to the colony
  • Issue Special Project: Orbital Clean-Up
Declare a state of crisis.
  • Trash Rings modifier added
  • Adds an Astrogarbage Unit planetary feature to the colony
  • Issue Special Project: Intense Orbital Clean-Up

Orbital Clean-Up Complete
A momentous day on [colony name] as the astrogarbage collectors have completed their final shift. As of this day, the trash rings surrounding [colony name] have been reduced by such a scale that they no longer pose a major threat to passing ships.

In the coming days, the work will continue on a smaller scale. It might be impossible to clean the orbit entirely, but at least the worst is over.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Veiled in Debris
 Is triggered only by:

Finishing the Orbital Clean-Up special project

Finally things can return to normal.
  • Trash Rings modifier removed
  • Unity Gained: 150-100000

Clear Skies
A historic celebration has erupted on [colony name] as the astrogarbage collectors' final shift has ended.

As of this day, the trash rings that once surrounded the planet are no longer detectable. Due to this success, the collectors have received resounding support to continue their operation, ensuring clear skies for generations to come.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Veiled in Debris
 Is triggered only by:

Finishing the Intense Orbital Clean-Up special project

A momentous day.
  • Trash Rings modifier removed
  • Clear Skies modifier added for 5 years, giving the following effects
    • Happiness +20%
    • Automatic Resettlement Destination Chance +10%
    • Stability +10
    • Unity from Jobs +50%

Ground Locked
After dismissing concerns over some runaway space garbage in orbit around [colony name], the situation has drastically escalated.

As the colonial scientists suggested, individual pieces of space trash have collided to create a halo of microtrash around the planet. This has made it extremely hard to transport resources and people in or out of the colony without risking collateral damage.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Veiled in Debris
  • Did not issue any special project
  • 60% chance
 Mean time to happen:

6 months

Oops.
  • Ground Locked modifier added for 5 years, giving the following effects
    • Happiness −15%
    • Automatic Resettlement Destination Chance −75%
    • Resources from Jobs −15%

Shooting Stars
After dismissing concerns over some runaway space garbage in orbit around [colony name], the issue seems to have resolved itself.

Thousands of 'shooting stars' have begun to fall en masse as the planet's strong gravitational pull brings the trash close enough to burn in the atmosphere. Nightly gatherings have become commonplace as people arrive to witness the unique display.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Veiled in Debris
  • Did not issue any special project
  • 40% chance
 Mean time to happen:

6 months

Time for some recreational stargazing.
  • Trash Rings modifier removed
  • Shooting Stars modifier added for 5 years, giving the following effects
    • Happiness +20%
    • Automatic Resettlement Destination Chance +15%
    • Pop Amenities Usage −15%
