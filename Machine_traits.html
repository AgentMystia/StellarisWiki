Machine traits

Machine traits are available to species with the Machine and Mechanical primary traits. Empires with such a founder species can change machine traits at any point unless they have the Life-Seeded, Voidforged or Shattered Ring origin. Those empires, as well as empires with a biological founder species must research the Machine Template System technology to change machine traits.

Climate preference traits

Each species has a climate preference trait that determines their base Habitability on the various types of planets, usually that corresponding to the type of their homeworld. Habitability traits can be changed through machine modification unless the species has the Waterproof trait. A pop can never have more than one Habitability trait.

Despite the values listed here, Machine and Mechanical traits granting minimum habitability means that habitability will never go below 50%. However, base habitability must still be increased to increase habitability above 50%.

Basic traits

Basic traits can be selected when first creating a species, or changed through machine modification. They can all be changed later through machine modification, a few of them being restricted to Mechanical or Machine species.

Organic empires must research higher tiers of robot technologies before they can add some of the more advanced traits to Mechanical pops.

Mechanical leviathan traits

Available only with the The Machine Age DLC enabled.

Mechanical leviathan traits can only be obtained by synthetic empires that haven't picked psionic ascension, or cybernetic empires with Integrated Anatomy tradition pick. They must also have researched one of the following: Quantum Neuro-Links for cybernetic empires, Binary Motivator for gestalt machine intelligence empires, or Artificial Administration for non-gestalt synthetic empires.

Defeating a mechanical leviathan for the first time while fulfilling the above conditions will grant an option to obtain Leviathan Tech Genesis technology as a permanent research option, and unlock the trait for pop template modification. Defeating further mechanical leviathans will also unlock the corresponding traits.
