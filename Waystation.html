Waystation
 Available only with the Nomads DLC enabled.

A waystation is a structure that allows Nomadic empires to exert influence over a system, analogous to starbases used by settled empires. Each system can only have one waystation and must be fully surveyed before the waystation can be built. Building a waystation takes a year and costs 100 Influence and either 250 Alloys if the empire uses a mechanical shipset or 125 Alloys and 438 Food if the empire uses a biological shipset. Upgrading a waystation has the same time and cost except for Influence which is halved. The number of waystations that an empire can support without penalty is determined by the Station Capacity.

If the system is owned by another empire, it requires at least High Intel on the system and building the waystation will give a −30 Opinion penalty for each waystation as well as the Demarcation casus belli. The Opinion penalty can be reduced by half if the waystation can cloak. Both penalties can be removed if the empires have a Wayline Pact, are in the same Federation, have the same overlord, one empire is the other's subject, or both empires are part of the Galactic Imperium and one of them is the Galactic Emperor. The Build Waystation contract will remove both the penalties and the High Intel requirement but only for the system where the contract was placed.

If two waystations are built in systems that are connected by a hyperlane or bypass, they will form a Wayline. Waystations initially appear as separate entries in the outlines but once they are part of a Wayline, the Wayline will appear in the outliner while the waystations will be removed but become visible when clicking on the Wayline they're part of. Waylines also allow nomadic empires to build megastructures in the system. Only the empire owning the waystations can benefit from a Wayline, but if two empires have a Wayline Pact, both of them will receive the benefits.

Unlike starbases, a waystation cannot repair docked fleets unless it has a Repair Bay module and if a waystation is defeated, it is destroyed instead of captured.

If the empire becomes settled, waystations in owned systems are removed while waystations in unowned systems are turned into outposts.

Level

Mammalian Wayhold

A waystation is auto-fitted with the latest components as soon as they are available to the empire. This is done automatically and has no additional cost for the empire - as such, this also does not affect a waystation's level upgrade cost.

The weapon slots are fitted with the most effective kinetic and energy weapons (though not necessarily by equal division). The utility slots are divided equally between shield and armor components (auto-fitting does not add hull components here), while the auxiliary slot uses standard auto-fitting of a shield capacitor or auxiliary fire-control (whatever is available in order of priority).

Modules

Depending on its level, a waystation can support up to 6 modules, which are visible on the waystation. The waystation will be described in the outliner depending on the majority module type. Each module takes 180 days to construct.

Economic support

Economic support modules can only be constructed if the system has at least one deposit with the required resource. With the exception of Resource Silos and defense modules, every module can only be built once. They gain a bonus if the empire has the Joyous Enlightenment or Middle Path modifier and the waystation is part of a wayline and in case of Middle Path, the bonus is reduced by half unless the system had one of the origin special projects.
