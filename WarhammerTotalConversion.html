Description

Warhammer 40k Total conversion is mod that changes Stellaris into the Warhammer 40k Universe

Release date: 01.12.2017 for the new version


In the future there is no peace, no justice and no hope. Only war.

Will you fight for the Emperor's glory or a Xenos race?

Choose wisely or face exterminatus

How to play

Read before playing!

Use only this one mod. No other mods. No other mods I made since everything is already here. Other mods will cause bugs.

Singleplayer:

  1. Choose Imperium. Only Imperium.
  2. Choose insane difficulty and aggressive AI

Multiplayer:

  1. Someone must! choose Imperium.
  2. I recommend someone to choose:
    • Eye of terror
    • Ghazkull
  3. Choose insane difficulty and aggressive AI

Current Status

  • Added Static Galaxy with almost 1200 solar systems
  • 1000 already owned systems with planet class/units/buildings etc. for example Vraks Prime - Armoury world with few IG regiments, big fortifications and Huge armoury
  • Added 35 Prescripted Empires
  • Added 37 new planet types (hive, forge etc.)
  • All major factions now have their own ship sets
  • Added unique armies for every Race
  • New buildings and technologies
  • New crisis such as Tyranids hive fleet and Warp Storms
  • dozens of new events (black crusades, age of apostasy etc.)
  • new ship class/special ships for example craftworld, eternal cruseder etc.
  • new animations for example Planet Killer firing his armageddon guns or Blackstone Fortress use laser to destroy planet
  • New Music - Galaxy Aflame, HMKIDS and from TTS YT series
  • many more smaller changes to make the game feel like Warhammer 40k
