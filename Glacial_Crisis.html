Glacial Crisis is a colony event chain that has a very small chance to trigger 12 years after any colony has been founded on a Cold planet. It can only happen once per game and cannot happen to empires with the Wilderness origin.

Glacial Crisis
The latest climate report from [colony name] has come to a concerning conclusion. Inexplicably, the surface temperature on the frozen planet has been rising at an alarming rate.

If we fail to identify the reason for the ecosystem's rapid deterioration, the situation could lead to catastrophic melting of the planet's vast glaciers.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Not capital world
  • Not Mastery of Nature
 Mean time to happen:

12 years

Find out why the planet is heating.
  • Issue Special Project: Melting Surface
It's nothing to worry about.

Cracks in the Ice
The escalation of temperatures on [colony name] is accelerating dramatically. This has caused substantial fractures within the icy surface layers, unveiling a vast subsurface ocean.

Fortunately, our investigations have identified a potential cause for this phenomenon. Due to our presence, the unique atmospheric shield safeguarding the planet has begun to exhibit critical fragility and could potentially collapse. Implementing stringent measures to curb our production would minimize our ecological impact. It might be enough to solve the issue.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Glacial Crisis
 Is triggered only by:

Finishing the Melting Surface special project

Revise our production targets.
  • Rising Water modifier added, giving the following effects: Habitability −10%
  • Halted Production modifier added, giving the following effects: Resources from Jobs −25%
It is about time the ice age ended.
  • Snowball Effect modifier added, giving the following effects:
  • Habitability −20%
  • Resources from Jobs +25%

Vast Floodplains
In the wake of our neglect to address the rapidly deteriorating ecological state of [colony name], the situation has spiraled into an unprecedented cataclysm.

The disintegration of vast ice sheets has given rise to sprawling oceans, leaving behind immense devastation and upheaval. By building flood barriers we can protect our remaining cities, but more unforeseeable consequences still linger on the horizon.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Glacial Crisis
 Is triggered only by:

The Melting Surface special project expires or is not issued

The planet is flooding!
  • A pop dies
  • Adds a Growing Floodplains blocker

End of an Ice Age
A new climatic era begins as the last vestiges of [colony name]'s glaciers sink into the ocean. The disappearance of the permafrost has uncovered an expansive underwater realm, filled with untapped resources.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Cracks in the Ice
  • Colony has the Snowball Effect modifier
 Mean time to happen:

24 months

Time to hit the beach.
  • Planet becomes an Ocean World
  • Adds the Rich Seabed planetary feature
  • Snowball Effect modifier removed
  • If the founder species has the Aquatic or Waterproof trait gain a large amount of Unity

Core Meltdown
Our attempt to limit our emissions on [colony name] has not yielded promising results. We have been only marginally successful in addressing the ongoing ecological crisis, and geographical lowlands are coming under threat due to the rising sea levels.

At present, our population centers remain shielded from immediate impact, but it is almost certain planet-wide meltdown cannot be avoided.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Cracks in the Ice
  • Colony has the Rising Water and Halted Production modifiers
 Mean time to happen:

12 months

Try to mitigate the damages.
  • Adds a Growing Floodplains blocker
Declare a state of emergency!
  • Adds a Growing Floodplains blocker
  • Halted Production modifier removed
  • Ecological Emergency modifier added, giving the following effects: Resources from Jobs −50%
  • Issue Special Project: Climate Crisis

Secrets of the Permafrost
As the meltdown of the planet [colony name] continues, a fascinating discovery was made beneath the permafrost. An astonishingly abundant polar reservoir of minerals and rare resources lies waiting, beckoning us with untold wealth and untapped potential.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Vast Floodplains
 Mean time to happen:

12 months

Jackpot. Start drilling!
  • Adds an Ore-Rich Caverns planetary feature
The permafrost has suffered enough.
  • Unity Gained: 150-100000

Climate Stabilized
We have reached an environmental breakthrough.

The innocuous byproducts generated through the combustion of our fuels harbor an insidious side-effect when coming to contact with [colony name]'s bacterial clouds. We now know that these clouds play a vital role in retaining the planet's natural biorhythm by working as a solar shield. As the bacteria slowly begun to diminish, we inadvertently exacerbated the effect of our emissions.

Armed with this newfound knowledge, resolute measures have been swiftly enacted to restore the equilibrium.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Core Meltdown
 Is triggered only by:

Finishing the Climate Crisis special project

At last, the climate is stable again.
  • Rising Water modifier removed
  • Ecological Emergency modifier removed
  • Unity Gained: 250-1000000
  • Physics Research Gained: 500-1000000

Unfrozen Pathogen
A disconcerting development has unfolded at our polar mining station on [fcolony name]. Workers have succumbed to sudden bouts of unconsciousness, necessitating the implementation of a stringent no-pass zone for their safety.

Initial investigations suggest the emergence of a dormant virus, awoken as its icy confines were disturbed. The true nature and characteristics of this awakened pathogen remain uncertain, leaving us grappling with a multitude of unanswered questions. The extent of its transmissibility and the potential danger it poses to the people remains undetermined.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Secrets of the Permafrost
  • Colony gained the Ore-Rich Caverns planetary feature
  • Not Machine or Mechanical founder species
  • 60% chance
 Mean time to happen:

8 months

Take no chances. Sanitize the area.
  • A pop dies
  • Murderous Precautions modifier added for 12 months, giving the following effects: Happiness −25%
Keep the quarantine up.
  • Ancient Virus modifier added for 24 months, giving the following effects:
  • Pop Growth Speed −10%
  • Automatic Resettlment Destination Chance −50%

Polar Riches
In the hollowed depths of the polar regions of [colony name], a mining team has made a discovery of monumental proportions. Veiled beneath layers of ice and snow, colossal crystals reminiscent of majestic glaciers were found.

These extraordinary formations, forged under immense pressure over untold ages, stand as a testament to the unlimited potential we have yet to uncover.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Secrets of the Permafrost
  • Colony gained the Ore-Rich Caverns planetary feature
  • 40% chance
 Mean time to happen:

8 months

What an incredible find.
  • Adds the Crystallized Ice Field planetary feature
