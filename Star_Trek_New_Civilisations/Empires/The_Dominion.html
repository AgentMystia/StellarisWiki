Description

The Dominion is a grouping of multiple species in the Gamma Quadrant.

Established thousands of years ago by the shapeshifters known as the Founders, the Vorta administer the day to day workings of the Dominion, and direct the fearsome Jem'Hadar cloned soldiers against its enemies.
