Description

Klingons are a warrior species from the planet Qo'noS, known for their strength and aggressive adherence to honour and tradition.

The Klingons were first united as one under Kahless the Unforgettable over 1100 years ago, who drove the invading Hur'q from Qo'noS.

The peculiarities of Klingon culture generally leads to poor relations with other races, and a need to conquer and expand as resources are used up.
