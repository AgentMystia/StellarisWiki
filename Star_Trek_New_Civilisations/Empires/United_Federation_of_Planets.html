Formation

When playing as United Earth, Tellar, Vulcan or Andoria, you can bring the canon Federation founding nations together to form the United Federation of Planets.

To form the Federation you must form an Alliance with at least 2 nations from the following list:

  • United Earth
  • Vulcan High Command -or- Confederacy of Surak
  • Andorian Empire
  • United Planets of Tellar

Once this condition is met, and provided that the Alliance is not at war and the Xindi or Terra Prime event chains are not in progress, you can form the United Federation of Planets by triggering the formation Agenda..

If the Alliance contains any other nations, these will also be offered the choice to join the newly founded Federation as Member Worlds, but may refuse.

Accession: Growing the Federation

Federation Accession Process

After the Federation has been founded, you can incorporate other nations as Member Worlds via the Federation Accession process.

Your subjects and all nations that are in an Alliance or have Association Status with the Federation are Accession candidates.

To view a list of Federation Accession candidates, go to the Institutions menu (via the Edicts menu) and open the Federation Council. Select the option to view accession candidates and select a candidate to be begin the process.

Your selected candidate will be offered membership but may refuse. This decision is influenced positively by the following factors:

  • The candidate has a Xenophile ethic
  • The candidate is a (canonical) Federation Founder
  • You have a higher relative power than the candidate
  • The candidate's opinion of you

Accession will take several years, or decades depending on the relative size of the target nation, but can be accelerated via the following:

  • Assign multiple Envoys to Improve Relations with the candidate
  • Take the Shared Destiny Ascension Perk
  • Increase Accession Focus level in the Accession Situation - higher levels cost more Influence
  • Build the Political Facilitation Office Overlord Holding on Member World planets

When the nation is ready to join as a Member World you will receive a notification and can complete the Accession Process through the Federation Council Institution.

Multiple Accessions can be conducted simultaneously but the process will be slower.

Member World Subjects

Member Worlds are a Specialist Subject type. A Member World will initially retain ownership of its colonies, fleets and home world but will be gradually integrated over time.

At Tier 1, Member Worlds retain control of their inhabited systems, colony worlds, fleets, home system and home world.

At Tier 2, Member Worlds retain control of their home system and home world only.

At Tier 3, Member Worlds retain control of their home world only.

Member Worlds pay Science and Strategic Resource taxes to the UFP, and receive Basic Resources in return. The tax rates are fixed can never be adjusted.

Member Worlds do not participate independently in galactic diplomacy.
