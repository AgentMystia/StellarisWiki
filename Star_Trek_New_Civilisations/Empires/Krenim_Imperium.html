Description

The Krenim are a humanoid species from the Delta Quadrant.

Physiologically humanoid, they are distinguished by hardened skeletal membranes found on their forehead. The Krenim are mainly known for their chroniton and temporal research, and their tendency to find themselves involved in border disputes with neighbouring species.
