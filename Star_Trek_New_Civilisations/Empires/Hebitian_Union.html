Description

The Hebitians are a quasi-reptilian humanoid species native to the planet Hebitia.

They appear to have evolved from a creature with both reptilian and mammalian traits, with both gray or grayish green skin tones and dark black hair. Elongated vertebrae extend along their necks, and ridges extend along their forehead.

The Hebitians are a spiritual people with a rich cultural history.

Alternatives

The following empires can replace the Hebitian Union in certain circumstances:

  • Cardassian Union, when selected in Empire List.
