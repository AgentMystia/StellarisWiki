Description

The Cardassians are a quasi-reptilian humanoid species native to the planet Cardassia.

They appear to have evolved from a creature with both reptilian and mammalian traits, with both gray or grayish green skin tones and dark black hair. Elongated vertebrae extend along their necks, and ridges extend along their forehead.

The Cardassians have a general belief that they are superior to other races in the galaxy and all should serve them.

Alternatives

The following empires can replace the Cardassian Union in certain circumstances:

  • Hebitian Union, when selected in Empire List.
