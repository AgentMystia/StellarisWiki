Description

The Antedians are a bipedal species with a piscine appearance. Native to the large oceans of Antede III, they are somewhat reclusive.

Whilst Antedeans have discovered warp, they find spaceflight extremely traumatic with most taken to surviving the ordeal by entering a self-induced catatonic state.
