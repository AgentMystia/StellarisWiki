Description

The Son'a are a species of humanoids from the planet Ba'ku. Nomadic in nature after their self imposed exile, they maintain outposts in space rather than colonising worlds.

The Son'a are considered a narcissistic and materialistic people, and they put great value in precious metals, rare jewels and other materials. They were often thought to raid inhabited worlds in search of these resources and also for slave labor, especially females.
