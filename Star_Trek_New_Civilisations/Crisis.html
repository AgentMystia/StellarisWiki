Star Trek New Civilisations/Crisis

Game modification: Star Trek New Civilisations

A crisis is a major story event that threatens all or many empires in the game.

List of Crises

Name Description Available When
Borg Invasion The Borg attempt to assimilate the whole map. Based on events in Star Trek: The Next Generation. Map Types:
  • Alpha/Beta Quadarant
  • Gamma Quadrant
  • Alpha Octant
  • Birth of the Federation

Must Not Exist:

  • Borg Collective
Dominion Invasion The Dominion attempt to conquer the Alpha and Beta Quadrants. Based on events in Star Trek: Deep Space Nine. Map Types:
  • Alpha/Beta Quadarant
  • Delta Quadrant
  • Alpha Octant
  • Birth of the Federation

Must Not Exist:

  • The Dominion
Mirror Universe Invasion Terrans from the Mirror Universe attempt to cross into the Prime Universe and conquer it. Game Settings:
  • Prime Universe

Must Exist:

  • United Earth / United Federation of Planets
  • Klingon Empire
  • Cardassian Union
Prime Universe Infiltration As the Terran Empire, dispatch fleets to invade the Prime Universe. Game Settings:
  • Mirror Universe

Must Be a Player:

  • Terran Empire
Unity Device The reemergence of the Chodak threatens galaxy-wide calamity. Game Settings:
  • Prime Universe
Fluidic Space The Undine (Species 8472) invade from Fluidic Space to conquer the galaxy. Must Not Exist:
  • Undine Vanguard
Andromedan Invasion Aliens from the Andromeda Galaxy launch an invasion. Must Not Exist:
  • Kelvan Empire
Sphere Builders The transdimensional beings who built the Delphic Expanse spheres seek to expand throughout the galaxy. Map Types:
  • Alpha/Beta Quadrant
  • Milky Way Galaxy

Must Not Exist:

  • Tuterian Ascendancy
