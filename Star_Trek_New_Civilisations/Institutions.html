Star Trek New Civilisations/Institutions

Game modification: Star Trek New Civilisations

Institutions group together a set of related policies and actions into a single view. Institutions may be unlocked based on empire type, by researching technologies, or through events. They represent organizations within or related to the empire's government, and help to operate mechanics unique to STNC. The Institution menu can be opened from the Edicts panel.
