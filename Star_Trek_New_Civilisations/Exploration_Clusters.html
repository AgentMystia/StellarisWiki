Star Trek New Civilisations/Exploration Clusters

Game modification: Star Trek New Civilisations

Exploration Clusters are locations from which the player can dispatch survey missions. Once dispatched, the surveying ship will disappear from the map and return when their mission has completed several months later.

A variety of survey outcomes are available, and several clusters provide unique event chains for the player to explore and interact with.

Clusters can be re-entered if previously withdrawn from via an edict costing 100 influence.
