Star Trek New Civilisations/Megastructures

Game modification: Star Trek New Civilisations

Types of Megastructure

Megastructures in Star Trek New Civilisations generally represent noteworthy elements of space-based civilian or military infrastructure. Minor megastructures (or, "kilostructures") are typically built in a single stage and usually provide a bonus to either the planet they orbit, all planets in the same system, or ships passing through that system. A few larger megastructures are built in multiple stages, or can be expanded as new technologies are discovered. There are some inhabitable megastructures, but they are not generally available - usually these are limited to a specific faction (e.g. Borg Collective) or must be unlocked via a tradition or technology.

Megastructure Type Availability Effects Build Restrictions
Trade Station Civilian Infrastructure Technology
  • Generates 10 Consumer Goods and 1 Latinum.
  • Interact with the station Administrator to purchase trade goods (each of which grants a unique empire-wide bonus) and rare exotic goods such as ship designs and weapon technologies.
  • Must be built on a trade deposit or colony.
  • Limit one per system.
Subspace Relay Civilian Infrastructure Technology
  • Generates 2 Physics Research and 4 Unity.
  • Reduces Empire Size from population and increases Unity output on all planets in the system.
  • Limit one per system
Medical Flotilla Civilian Infrastructure Tradition: Medical
  • Generates 5 Society Research (10 after upgrade).
  • Adds 4 Medical Worker jobs to colony (6 after upgrade).
  • Increases Population Growth on the planet it orbits.
  • Must be built on a colony.
Particle Fountain Civilian Infrastructure Technology
  • If built over a Minerals deposit, produces 25x the original deposit (max 100).
  • If built over a Dilithium deposit, produces 5x the original deposit (max 20).
  • Must be built on a mined Minerals or Dilithium deposit.
  • Limit one per system.
Subspace Telescope Military Infrastructure Technology
  • Sets system sensor range to 8 and hyperlane detection range to 12.
  • Increases Base Intel by 10.
  • Limit one per system.
Baryon Sweep Array Military Infrastructure Technology
  • Provides 100 Baryon Sweep Capacity (250 after upgrade).
  • If Baryon Sweep Capacity is equal or greater than Used Naval Capacity, reduces ship upkeep by 10%. If less, the upkeep reduction is multiplied by (Baryon Sweep Capacity / Used Naval Capacity).
  • Limit one per system.
Traffic Control Station Civilian Infrastructure Technology
  • Decreases warp windup and winddown time by 10%.
  • Increases warp speed by 10% (for ships departing this system).
  • Increases sublight speed by 10%.
  • Must be built outside the system gravity well.
  • Limit one per system.
Transwarp Conduit Bypass Ascension Perk: Post-warp Alternatives
  • Serves as a gateway allowing fleets to move instantly from one friendly conduit to another, anywhere in the galaxy.
  • Must not be within 2 hyperlane jumps from another Transwarp Conduit.
  • Must be built outside the system gravity well.
  • Limit one bypass gateway per system.
Graviton Catapult Bypass Ascension Perk: Post-warp Alternatives
  • Used to transport a fleet instantly from the catapult location, to any other system within 200 lightyears.
  • Must not be within 100 lightyears of another Graviton Catapult.
  • Must not be within 30 lightyears of a black hole.
  • Limit one bypass gateway per system.
Seed Vault Ship Civilian Infrastructure Technology
  • Enter orbit of inhabited planets and complete the sample gathering Decision to increase Society research produced by the Seed Vault.
  • Costs 0.01 Food for each planet that has gathered seeds.
  • Generates 0.1 Society Research for each planet that has gathered seeds.
  • Limit one per empire.
Asteroid Prison Habitable Tradition: Order
  • A habitat focussed on penal mining labour.
  • Reduces Crime on other colonies.
  • Must be built on an asteroid.
  • Limit one per sector.
Fleet Yards Military Infrastructure Technology
  • Generates 10 Engineering Research (15 after first upgrade, 25 after second upgrade).
  • Provides 3 shipyard buildings (6 after first upgrade, 8 after second upgrade).
  • This Fleet Yards ship build speed +25% (+50% after second upgrade).
  • This Fleet Yards ship starting experience +100.
  • Limit one per system.
Fluidic Gateway Explorable Special Event
  • Generates 10 Physics Research.
  • Similar to an exploration cluster. Allows an empire to send science ships to explore the fluidic realm where everything is totally fine and there is nothing to worry about...
  • Cannot be built in a system with any colony worlds.
  • Limit one per empire.
Suliban Helix Habitable Suliban empires only.
  • A small habitat.
  • Cannot be built on a habitable world.
  • Limit one habitat per system.
Hunters Lodge Habitable Hirogen empires only.
  • A small habitat.
  • Must be built on an asteroid.
  • Limit one habitat per system.
Undine Reef Habitable Undine empires only.
  • A small habitat.
  • Limit one habitat per system.
Planetary Starbase Civilian or Military Infrastructure Technology
  • Can be built around inhabited worlds and has many of the same options as a system starbase, allowing for either civilian or military specialisation.
  • Can host habitation modules which increase the usable size of the planet they orbit - very similar to Stellaris Orbital Rings.
  • Cannot built on primitive worlds, ringworlds, or habitats.
  • Limited by System Starbase Limit.
Borg Subcomplex Habitable Borg empires only.
  • A small habitat.
  • Cannot build on asteroids, moons, stars, or habitats.
  • Limited by Subcomplex Capacity.
Borg Unicomplex Habitable Borg empires only.
  • On completion, removes all planets and the star from the system.
  • Vast habitable Borg space stations. Each Unicomplex system can host four Unicomplex sections.
  • Must be built in a system with one star (not a black hole).
  • System must contain at least one planet.
  • Cannot be built in a system with any colony worlds or other megastructures.
  • Limited by Unicomplex Capacity and System Unicomplex Capacity.
Tuterian Sphere Special Tuterian empires only.
  • Used by the Tuterian Sphere Builders to expand their domain into the galaxy as we know it.
  • Makes worlds within its influence habitable to Tuterians and less so for others.
  • Starts with a small district limit but can be expanded through planetary decisions.
  • Must be built in a system with Spatial Anomalies or neighboring a system with Spatial Anomalies.
  • Cannot be built in a system adjacent to another Sphere (by map adjacency).
  • Limit one habitat per system.
Theta Radiation Dump Civilian Infrastructure Civic: Theta Polluters
  • Provides 50 Theta Radiation Dumping Capacity (100 after first upgrade, 200 after second upgrade).
  • Colonies in adjacent systems (by map adjacency) suffer -5% Habitability, 5% Pop Growth Speed, and -5% Happiness (10% after first upgrade, 20% after second upgrade).
  • Cannot be built in a system with any colony worlds.
  • Cannot be built in a system bordering an empire that has a theta radiation ban treaty.
  • Limit one per system.
