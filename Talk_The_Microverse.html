The Microverse

The small universe on the other side of the astral rift

Each time an Astral Scar is surveyed after both the Astral Harvesting and Rift Sphere technologies have been researched and the empire already has an Astral Scar within borders there is an 80% chance to issue the Astral Scar Instability special project. The system will gain −50% Sublight Speed and −100% Trade Value until the special project is finished, which takes 60 days. If the special project is not completed it expires in 3 years and will not be offered again. Finishing the special project will bring the option to either open the The Microverse astral rift or seal it and receive a large amount of Astral Threads and a small amount of Physics Research.

The astral rift has the following chapters:

1. Acceleration, gives 100 Dark Matter, proceed to Srinking Space.

2. Shrinking Space, gives small astral threads reward (40, 50, or 60), proceed to Temporal Leak.

3 Temporal Leak, gives 24x physics output (500~1 000 000)

  • 3 "Synchronize with the Fleet.", proceed to Emergency
  • 6 "Repolarize the hull.", costs 10 Astral Threads, proceed to Unbirthing

4b. Unbirthing, gives 24x physics output (500~1 000 000)

  • 3 "Approach the closest star system.", proceed to Stardust
  • 6 "Observe from the edge of the universe.", proceed to End of the Beginning (Cosmic Horizon)

5. Stardust, gives medium astral threads reward (90, 100, or 110)

  • "There may never be another chance.", proceed to End of the Beginning (Star)

6a. End of the Beginning (Cosmic Horizon), gives 48x physics output (1000~1 000 000), small astral threads reward (40, 50, or 60), ends the rift

6b. End of the Beginning (Star), gives Empire Modifier "A Star is Born" for 10 years (+10% Physics Research), small astral threads reward (40, 50, or 60), ends the rift

6c. Emergency, scientist becomes 5 years younger, gives 96x physics output (2000~2 000 000), ends the rift
