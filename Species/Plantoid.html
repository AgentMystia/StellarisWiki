Species/Plantoid

Note: This is a subpage to list Species portraits.

Plantoids are plant lifeforms that have gained sapience, thus their appearence is modeled after real-world plants. Plantoid shipset and city appearance are accessible only with Plantoids Species Pack active. With Plantoids Species Pack, plantoid and fungoid species gain access to unique traits, civics, and origins.
