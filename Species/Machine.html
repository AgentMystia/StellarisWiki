Species/Machine

Note: This is a subpage to list Species portraits.

Machines are not a naturally evolved species but rather an artificial one that can be constructed with the required technology or if by starting as a machine empire.

This species type actually encompasses two distinct species classes: Machine and Mechanical. The former consists of highly advanced machines that have broken free from their organic creators' control; they could be either Gestalt or Individualist, and their creators may have either perished, been assimilated, or been carefully preserved. The latter refers to complex robots created by organic empires, and synthetic bodies into which the organics' neural patterns have been transfered. The initial species template, created by the Artificial Workforce technology, the Mechanist origin, or the Synthetic Evolution ascension perk, will always match the class of the empire's founder species.

The Machine Age DLC is required to start the game as an individualistic Machine empire and to use the Synthetic shipset.
