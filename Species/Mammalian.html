Species/Mammalian

Note: This is a subpage to list Species portraits.

The defining characteristic of mammalians is their fur. Many draw inspiration from real Earth mammals, which is why some resemble civilized animals in space.

Most mammalian portraits have a unique dialogue when targeted with a diplomacy insult.
