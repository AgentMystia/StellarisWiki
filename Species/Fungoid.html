Species/Fungoid

Note: This is a subpage to list Species portraits.

Fungoids have quite imaginative designs, featuring experimental concepts such as a fungus taking over the body of another creature, or a necromorphic race where the fungus slowly decays its host. With the Plantoids Species Pack, fungoid and plantoid species gain access to unique traits, civics, and origins.
