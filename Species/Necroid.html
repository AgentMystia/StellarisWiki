Species/Necroid

Note: This is a subpage to list Species portraits.

Available only with the Necroids DLC enabled.

Necroids center around the theme of death and those who cheated it. The portraits feature diverse design concepts and backstories, such as a species which inhabits a completely sealed suit, or an alien whose cells just keep dividing through genetic manipulation.
