Once the Mid-Game year has been reached empires with the On the Shoulders of Giants origin that finished excavating all archaeological sites in their home system will be given a unique event chain. Finishing the event chain will replace the Spurred by the Past empire modifier with a more powerful one.

The event chain has two mutually-exclusive stories determined when the final chapter of the last archaeological site has been excavated. Both the steps required and the rewards are identical in both routes.

Cold Trail

During a routine system scan one of our sensors picked up a small - but strange - energy signature. It's quite likely trace exhaust from a fusion drive or something similar, but the signature doesn't match anything we've encountered so far, and it appears to be remarkably old - several thousands of years! One theory amongst our scientists is that the fusion drive trail might belong to the Benefactors.

We should assign our astro-science department to this research project as soon as we can.
 
Trigger conditions:

80% chance

Mean time to happen:

5 days

Fascinating.
  • Issue Special Project: Pathfinding
  • Event chain is named Intercepting History in the situation log

Trail of the Victor

One of our sensors recently picked up a strange energy signature in our home system. It was almost dismissed as a glitch at first; upon closer inspection the reading turned out to be an ancient energy trail from a fusion-based propulsion drive.

Deeper analysis is needed to know more, but it is possible that this trail was left by the Relentless when they left our system thousands of years ago.

We should investigate this further.
 
Trigger conditions:

20% chance

Mean time to happen:

5 days

OK

  • Event chain is named Relentless Pursuit in the situation log

Finding the Benefactors

The old fusion drive trail turned out be quite challenging to analyze due to its considerable age. Eventually the science department managed to calculate a likely navigation vector, and establish that the trail was pointing to a hitherto unknown system not many jumps away.

The destination has been made available, and we should consider sending a science vessel there to investigate further.

Picking up the Trail

Despite its remarkable age, our science department have managed to analyze the drive trail sufficient to ascertain its exit vector. This effectively means that we can pursue the demons of our past.

Has enough time passed to quell their violent nature completely, or are we to discover a new threat? A new route has been plotted, and we can pursue it whenever we want.

These Ravaged Fields

Even after the first, broad sensor reading of the [current system name] system it became clear that something very violent and destructive occurred here eons ago.

Broken spaceship parts and debris are scattered throughout the system, and the local void is teeming with energy residue from weaponized lasers and fusion explosions.

In this silent graveyard a single signal has been isolated, and it's coming from a small space station nearby.

The Silent Freighter

On entering the system, our sensors immediately picked up multiple anomalies.

The most prominent reading was a gigantic energy cloud which - upon closer inspection - turned out to be hundreds of smaller fusion drive trails like the one previously discovered. An absolutely massive fleet must've come to a halt here for some reason.

Next to this 'fleet parking lot' our scans picked up another kind of reading: a derelict transport ship. This freighter appears to be as old as the energy signatures, but traces of its own signature differs from that of the lost fleet.

We should inspect the abandoned ship closer.

Raided and Hunted

The signal we picked up originated from an emergency pod aboard the space station. Due to severe damage, the dead station left few clues to what happened here. Our scientists have managed to piece together snippets of data, in order to form a narrative.

This station was a research facility, and is where the Mind-Wipe technology was invented - the same technology used to diminish our ancestors. The station and its protective fleet were engaged by some unknown force, after which the technology was most likely stolen.

One particularly interesting message has been translated - radio chatter from the enemy fleet:

'They're trying to escape! Follow them. Use their own weapon against them. See how they like it.'

We've picked up a new fusion drive trail and established an exit vector based on it.

The Maker's Might

The freighter contained a log. Though damaged by time, some data could still be recovered.

The freighter had been intercepted by the massive fleet. Unclear as to how or why, the Captain of the freighter ended up conversing with the Admiral of the Relentless fleet. Apparently they spoke for weeks, but what they spoke of remains unknown - although the phrase 'nature of power' recurs frequently.

The fleet suddenly departed, leaving the freighter unscathed. The last entry in the log was a quote from the Admiral: 'No, leave them. Destruction is easy. Real power is the ability to create.'

We have analyzed the fleet trails and may take up our pursuit again.

The Tyrant's Rest

This is where the fusion drive trail ends. It's quite likely that any remnants of the Relentless species - if there are any - will be found here.

So far the only sensor reading of any particular interest comes from what appears to be some kind of small space station. An energy pulse radiates from it at set intervals, but apart from that there's no sign of any activity.

We can proceed to investigate immediately.

Final Rest

The primitive species found in this system is none other than the Thwaa'Phyr - known more commonly in our culture as the Benefactors.

Our xenologists are unsure of the details, but everything indicates that they somehow fell victim to their own mind-wiping technology, and were left on this planet to fend for themselves. No one knows the implications of this fate better than we do.

We should consider our next step carefully.

Blissfully Ignorant

The scientists have finished their investigation of the old space station. The entire structure is a big cannon of sorts. Eons ago it triggered and fired a pulse at the Relentless homeworld - a pulse that we, the [founder species name], know full well: a Mind-Wipe pulse.

In a quest to redeem themselves from their past sins, the Relentless - now known as the Vemetosha - used the weapon on themselves, effectively diminishing their entire civilization.

The magnitude of such a decision - and the follow-through - stands as testament to how very seriously the Relentless wanted to atone. Can we forgive them?

Closure

Sometimes the events of the universe unfold in a most poetic fashion. Once the undisputable masters of our ancestors, the Thwaa'Phyr species eventually found themselves struck by the same fate as our ancestors.

Whatever their intention might have been, we must acknowledge the fact that to some degree, they tried to redeem their desperate and excessive act.

Now that eons have passed, the sides have been reversed, and we have returned their favor.

Just as the [founder species name] species once did, now it's time for the Thwaa'Phyr to experience what it means to stand on the shoulders of giants.

Not the Enemy

A dark chapter of in the history of the universe has ended, and we have written a new one - a brighter chapter. Our benevolent project initiated a ripple effect in our society, each member feeling a sense of purpose and good intent. Maybe space isn't such a dark place after all - the unfathomably vast darkness of the void notwithstanding.
