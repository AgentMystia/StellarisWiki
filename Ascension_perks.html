Ascension perks
The Shroud

Ascension perks grant powerful, empire-wide benefits that can shape the course of a civilization's development, opening paths to greater strength, resilience, or transcendence. They are unlocked by finishing an entire tradition tree or acquiring the Ascension Theory technology. An empire can have up to 8 ascension perks.

List of ascension perks

Some perks require a number of other ascension perks to be taken first. Tier 0 ascension perks can be taken from the start. Tier 1 perks require 1 previous ascension perk to be taken, tier 2 perks require 2 previous ascension perks, and tier 3 perks require 3 previous ascension perks. The higher the AI weight, the more likely an AI empire will select that ascension perk.

If the Shadows of the Shroud DLC is enabled, most ascension perks also grant +30 Attunement with certain Shroud patrons.

Ascensions

Ascensions are special ascension perks that unlock a tradition tree, as well as an agenda with the same name that unlocks technologies required to advance through the tradition tree. Each empire can only take one of these perks, and only if they have already selected two ascension perks and have an empty tradition slot available.

Some civics and origins affect the availability of ascensions:

  • Empires with a
    Natural Design

    Innate Design
    civic cannot pursue ascension and therefore cannot pick any of these perks.
  • Synthetic ascension:
    • Empires with the Synthetic Fertility origin can adopt the Synthetics tradition tree without Synthetic Evolution, and cannot take any of these perks or pursue other ascensions.
  • Cybernetic ascension:
    • Empires with the Cybernetic Creed origin can adopt the Cybernetics tradition tree without The Flesh is Weak, and cannot take any of these perks or pursue other ascensions.
    • Empires with the Augmentation Bazaars civic can only pursue Cybernetic ascension, and cannot pursue other ascensions.
    • Empires with the Hard Reset origin can initially only pick The Flesh is Weak. If the founder species gains the De-Cyberized trait it can no longer pick The Flesh is Weak but unlocks the other ascensions. A Cyberized Hard Reset empire can adopt the Cybernetics tradition tree without the ascension perk.
  • Biological ascension:
    • Empires with the Wilderness origin can only pursue Biological ascension, cannot pursue other ascensions, and can only select Purity options during the ascension situation.
    • Empires with the Evolutionary Predators origin can only pursue Biological ascension, cannot pursue other ascensions, and can only select Mutation options during the ascension situation.
    • Empires with the Clone Army origin cannot pick the Biomorphosis ascension perk until they complete the Genetic Crossroads event chain. If they complete the event chain, they can pick the Biomorphosis ascension perk as their first ascension perk. Clone Army empires with Clone Potential path that pursue Biological ascension can only select Cloning options during the ascension situation.
  • Psionic ascension:
    • Empires with the Teachers of the Shroud or Endbringers origin can adopt the Psionics tradition tree without Mind Over Matter, and cannot take any of these perks or pursue other ascensions.

Ambitions

Ambitions are special ascension perks that unlock an ambition progression. Each empire can only take one of these perks, and only if they have already selected 3 ascension perks. They cannot be taken if the empire has the Defender of the Galaxy ascension perk, the Endbringers origin, or is the Galactic Emperor. They also cannot be taken by Minamar Specialized Industries.

Ambitions are not inherited by sectors released as vassals, but technologies unlocked by ambitions can be. AI empires are 5 times more likely to pick an ambition if no other empire has that ambition, and at least one empire picked another ambition.

Empires with the Shroud-Forged origin are locked out of the Galactic Nemesis perk if they completed the Forged by the Shroud situation by supporting the Shroud-Forged. If they completed the situation by suppressing the Shroud-Forged instead, they can unlock it without any prior ascension perks.

Wanderlust

Available only with the Nomads DLC enabled.

Wanderlust is a special ascension perk available only to Nomadic empires, has no other requirements, and adds 8 missions to the situation log.

  • When the ascension perk is taken will give +1 Max Districts and −10% Leader Upkeep
  • After half of the missions are completed the ascension perk will give +2 Max Districts, −20% Leader Upkeep and −10% Tradition Cost

The missions require entering 80% of the systems from each of the following categories. If one of these categories has zero systems because of galaxy settings, the reward will be received once one of the systems from another category is entered.
