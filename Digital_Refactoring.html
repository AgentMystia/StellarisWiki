All empires that completed the Synthetization situation and finished the Synthetics tradition tree will receive the Digital Refactoring event chain in 6 months. The event chain keeps track of whether the empire favored people being uploaded into physical bodies or remote-controlling them. At the end of the event chain the empire will have the option to switch to a new authority set based on the choices made. With the Synthetic Fertility origin, the choice made during the Virtual Salvation event also counts.

Follow-up events

After the initial event there are two sets of 3 events each that can take place. One event from each set will happen.

Synthesization of the entire [founder species adjective] species has also meant a drastic shift in our housing practices; with minds resting easy in massive server farms, synthetic bodies can be stored in shelves. While this represents a cost-effective solution, some advocacy groups are raising some very real concerns.

The previously lucrative real-estate market could collapse entirely, leaving a considerable dent in our economy. Potentially more damaging, at least from a psychological perspective, is that people will no longer have a physical place to call home.
