Common planet unique buildings

These buildings, or some variation of them, are available to most empires regardless of their civics, origin, or ascension path.

Automation buildings

Automation buildings automate workforce for the main job types of rural districts (technicians, miners, farmers), freeing pops for other important jobs. The amount of automation buildings are limited to one per specialized district, with a maximum of three per planet.

Automated workforce is affected by output modifiers, but is not affected by most efficiency modifiers (with the exception of automated workforce efficiency, from e.g. Production Overseers), and costs 4 energy per 100 automated workforce.

  • With no terraforming technology, the planet class must match the primary species' planet class preference. Upkeep on such worlds is not increased.
