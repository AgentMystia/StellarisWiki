The Sacred Path is an event chain available to all empires with the same origin and will give them a number of permanent empire modifiers. The special projects can only appear in unowned systems but those systems can be taken over by any empire afterwards. Only arkships can research the special projects.

The Ancestral Star Charts
There is a place called Emergence. For our ancestors, it marked the beginning of the Sacred Path. It is a fitting place to meditate on our ancient texts before proceeding with our own version of the Pilgrimage. By taking some time to research now, we can better plan for the journey ahead.

Afterward, we will chart a course to the known Sacred Sites. We will have many choices to make along the way. But in theory, if we trust in the deities and our ancestors, the true Path will be revealed to us.

Divided Faith
High Pilgrims, Devoted Adepts, and Faithful Acolytes. These three distinct social groups form the hierarchy of spiritual practitioners. This is how it has always been. High Pilgrims lead the way, while Devoted Adepts commit themselves to the path, and the Faithful Acolytes support our endeavors through worship and physical toil.

However, schisms are forming aboard the Arkship [capital arkship name] due to the roles we have assumed since committing to accelerated interstellar travel. Some crew members believe their responsibilities outweigh those of others, and now each group is demanding to be recognized as the most essential.

The Breathvault
We have surveyed [celstial object name], a place of sacred memory. A sealed chamber, once used by early pilgrims to record their final thoughts and commit them to rare psionic crystals.

The vault is now full, echoing with wisdom, faith, and joy. But within those memories, there are also centuries of preserved fear, loathing, and doubt.

If members of the congregation were to gain access to these final moments, it may challenge their faith in the Path.

The Chasm
Our ancestors spoke true. There is in fact a beast below the surface of [celestial object name]. We are blessed that it poses no threat to us, for it is deceased. The remains of the creature are staggeringly huge. Bipedal, winged, covered in scales, and with considerably more ocular organs than we've encountered before on a single being. It is truly alien.

In an alcove near the entrance to the pit, we discovered a message carved into the cavernous rock. In the language of the ancients, it says:

"WE, THE LIGHT BRINGERS, CAST OUT THIS ABOMINATION. THE FOUL BEAST WILL ROT IN ETERNITY."

We surmise our ancestors were not warning us of the beast, but of the consequences for betrayal. This creature's body remains an example of their wrath.

Finishing the Sacred Site: The Chasm special project

Angering the progenitors is never wise.

No creature deserves such a fate.

Whatever its sins, it deserves to rest peacefully.

The texts claim some Pilgrims who gazed through the lens were met with life-changing visions. Others are said to have wept, or fallen silent for days.

The so-called Eye has been staring into the abyss for over 2,000 years. We have no way of knowing the true object of our ancestors' observations.

The Intelligence
Orbiting [celestial object name] drifts a structure our ancestors once marked upon their star charts. Within its quiet chambers, we discovered a guiding presence: an ancient Intelligence, tasked with answering the questions of those who would one day walk this path.

Time has not been kind, but its voice still stirs. Fragments of instruction spill from hidden emitters, half-coherent and reverent in tone: "Continue along the luminous thread... The dark between stars grows thin..."

Its messages arrive in splinters, looping and colliding with older verses. Yet beneath the static and decay, there remains intention. It was meant to guide us. And perhaps, if we listen with care, it still can.

The Mirror
We arrived at the coordinates, and were promptly contacted by a ship with identical encryption protocols to our own. Perplexingly, our external sensors could not detect another vessel, but the transmission was clear as if we were in visual range. When the bridge view-screen flickered into action, we were met with the image of our own [ruler title and name].

Before we could respond, the double spoke:

"Be not afraid, [ruler title], you are safe. I take this form as a respectful gesture. Your path is in flux, and the steps you take next will set things into motion that you can not yet comprehend.

You must eliminate heretics who do not follow the Path."

The image vanished. We do not know what just happened.

The Path has always been an integral part of our culture. It was ordained by our ancestors. But having seen what they saw, and with the privilege of modern science, do the texts still ring true? Or is our society destined to follow a new, less pious Path?

At this point we have to ask ourselves: is there any way we can come together again as a people, and live in harmony? The alternative could be devastating.

Everyone should have the freedom to practice their faith in their own way, but if our factionalism leads us to civil war then we must be absolutely certain of our convictions.

The Future is Bright Now
The past pulls us back, keeping us from evolving. Learning from history is one thing, but remaining there, never moving beyond it, is foolish.

We need not waste our potential for self-discovery. Now that we have shrugged off the chains of yesterday, we can move forward with unbridled passion.

No longer will we be governed by our technologically inferior ancestors. No longer will the Path be dictated by the cryptic crumbs laid by the deities. We choose our own Path, and nothing can stand in our way.

The Middle Path
It was never about the Path itself. What's most important is the journey we make together. Our people have followed this tradition for centuries, and yet we have never found, in any of our historical documents, the true 'purpose' of the Path. It may be lost to time.

We will remember the surviving stories of our ancestors, and the Pilgrimage they made, but we no longer need to follow their lead. We know more about life, the galaxy, and ourselves than they did. The truths they held are no longer relevant to us. We will chart our own path.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Our Traditions Define Us or The Future is Bright Now
  • Steadfast Path: Devout or Incomplete Texts: Supplement modifier
 Mean time to happen:

5 years

The Path was within us all along.
  • Middle Path modifier added

Embracing the Chaos
Everything is clearer now. The Sacred Sites of our ancestors are sickly sweet beacons of hope and faith. Those who came before us had no shame, and they didn't care who knew it.

Our reputation has been trivialized and tarnished. We have it in our power to sanctify new sites that will communicate our updated spiritual mission.

The deities are complex and misunderstood. Through the Path, they have taught us that conflict is a natural feature of our society. The challenges we overcome make us stronger. Now we will demonstrate our strength across the galaxy!
