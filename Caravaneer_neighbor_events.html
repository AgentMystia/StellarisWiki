Caravaneer neighbor events can take place while an empire has a colonized planet up to 5 hyperlanes away from the system housing the Caravaneer home system.

Slaves to the Slots

Frequent visits to [caravaneer home system] have resulted in many people on [colony name] becoming addicted to gambling. Many have turned to crime in order to pay for repeated trips.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Not Gestalt Consciousness
 Mean time to happen:

50 years

Disturbing news.
  • Problematic Gambling modifier added for 60 months, giving the following effects: Crime +15

Festival

A visit by a large number of [caravaneer species] from the [caravaneer home system] has resulted in a massive party on [colony name]. Unfortunately, things have gotten out of hand and the "party" now resembles a planet-wide riot.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Not Gestalt Consciousness
 Mean time to happen:

50 years

Unacceptable!
  • Festival Aftermath modifier added for 60 months, giving the following effects: Stability −5
  • 33% chance for the world to gain 300 pops from the caravaneer species a year later

New slot machines!

The [caravan home system] recently revealed a new range of slot machines, and the increased traffic has caused an economic boom on [colony name] as visitiors pass through.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Not Gestalt Consciousness
 Mean time to happen:

50 years

Excellent.
  • Hot Slots! modifier added for 60 months, giving the following effects: Trade from Jobs +15%

Acclimatization

Chor's Compass is a rowdy place which is not for the faint of heart. Due to frequent visits, the citizens of [colony name] have become accustomed to conditions there to an unprecedented level.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Not Gestalt Consciousness
  • Not Evolutionary Predators
  • Any organic Pop lacks the Resilient trait on a colony
 Mean time to happen:

100 years

Excellent.
  • All organic pops on a colony gain the Resilient trait

MegaCorpse Concert: Millions Dead!
[colony name] has been left in chaos after a concert by the Psi-Metal phenomenon Megacorpse got out of hand. Reports are scrambled due to the wide-spread devastation, but one thing they all agree on is that at the height of the performance frontbeing Kel-Azaan slammed its hyper-guitar into the stage with such force the entire area was destroyed.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Not Gestalt Consciousness
  • Colony does not have an uninhabitable moon
 Mean time to happen:

100 years

Brutal.
  • 200 random Pops die
  • The colony gets +20 Devastation
  • The colony gains an Impact Crater Blocker
  • The planet gets the modifier

MegaCorpse Concert: Millions Dead!
[colony name] has been left in chaos after a concert by the Psi-Metal phenomenon Megacorpse got out of hand. Reports are scrambled due to the wide-spread devastation, but one thing they all agree on is that at the height of the performance frontbeing Kel-Azaan punched a hole in the moon, destroying it utterly.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Not Gestalt Consciousness
  • Colony has an uninhabitable moon
 Mean time to happen:

100 years

Brutal.
  • 200 random Pops die
  • The colony gets +20 Devastation
  • The moon becomes a Shattered World
  • The moon gains a deposit of +8 Minerals
