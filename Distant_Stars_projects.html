The Xvan Labs
Please help with verifying or updating this infobox. It was last verified for version 3.12.

We have located the Xvan Labs - a hidden gathering of pyramidic structures built from an unknown material, submerged under layers of sand and dust. We will have to excavate the facilities before we can explore them, but if the rebels were correct in their assumptions, the weapons within will be worth their while.

 

Requirements:

  • Have at least 1 science ship with a scientist in orbit

Time to Research:

  • Completed in 140 days
On Success:
  • Triggers the Invisible Death event

Study the Ketlings on [planet name]
Please help with verifying or updating this infobox. It was last verified for version 3.12.

Sapient creatures have emerged from the scrap piles on planet name. Our scientists are curious whether these aliens, dubbed Ketlings, are descendants of the space-faring civilization that once spanned these systems, or if they have another origin entirely.

We should be able to choose any planet inhabited by autonomous Ketlings to conduct this study.

 

Requirements:

  • Have at least 1 science ship with a scientist in orbit
  • For the duration of the project, the planet must:
    • Have at least 1 Ketling pop
    • Be under the control of the original pre-FTL Ketlings or the Ketling Star Pack

Time to Research:

  • Completed in 180 days
On Success:
  • Triggers the The Origin of the Ketlings event
