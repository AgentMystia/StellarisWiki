Genocidal
  • Civic always revealed regardless of Intel
  • Cannot establish diplomatic pacts
  • Cannot release sectors as subjects
  • Cannot use the Galactic Market
  • Cannot join the Galactic Community
  • Cannot discover space fauna technologies
  • Cannot issue or accept contracts
  • Pops that are not explicitly allowed will be purged and cannot be assimilated
  • Total war casus belli enabled to and from empires whose founder species is not explicitly allowed
  • −1000 Opinion to and from empires whose founder species is not explicitly allowed
  • If Grand Archive is also enabled gain Unity for unlocking 3, 5 and 9 exhibit slots from each specimen category
  • If Shadows of the Shroud is also enabled gain Attunement with Eater of Worlds
  • If Galactic Paradons is also enabled and the empire is individualist start with the civic's council position unlocked
