Stratospheric Vents is a small colony event chain that has a very small chance to trigger 2 years after a colony has been founded on a planet with the Stratospheric Vents modifier. It can only happen once per game.

Stratospheric Vents
Now that crucial colonization efforts of [colony name] have been completed, [empire adjective] scientists have turned their attention towards its lofty spires.

These mineral formations appear to be entirely hollow, transporting gaseous substances from beneath the planet's crust all the way to the thermosphere. For an as yet unknown reason, these gases also attract Tiyanki and other spaceborne fauna to travel across great distances to this planet.

A more thorough research project would allow us to study this planetary wonder and expand our understanding of these incredible creatures, or perhaps to devise a method of claiming these precious gases for ourselves.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Not capital world
  • Colony has the Stratospheric Vents modifier
 Mean time to happen:

24 months

Study the vents.
  • Issue Special Project: Stratospheric Vents Study
There is no time for this.

Stratospheric Vents Studied
We have determined that the vents on [colony name] have formed over millions of years as superheated gas and sediments cool down and solidify. The remaining fumes in the lower thermosphere form clouds that the Tiyanki feed on, plowing through with unexpected grace.

This affords us a rare opportunity not only to observe the creatures in close proximity, but also increase the colony's appeal to extraplanetary visitors. Alternatively, the vents could be harnessed for exotic gas refining at the expense of driving away the fauna from their ancient feeding grounds.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Stratospheric Vents
 Is triggered only by:

Finishing the Stratospheric Vents Study special project

Use for tourism and research.
  • Untouched Stratospheric Vents modifier added
Redirect for refining.
  • Stratospheric Vent Refineries modifier added

Stratospheric Vent Platforms
The unique tourism opportunities on [colony name] could be enhanced greatly by building luxurious viewing platforms near the most active vents. This would allow visitors to get up close and personal with Tiyanki as our on-site researchers guide them through this one of a kind experience.

The more affluent patrons could join scientific sampling expeditions or even experience the thrill of setting foot on a live Tiyanki!
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Stratospheric Vents Studied
  • Colony has the Untouched Stratospheric Vents modifier
 Mean time to happen:

24 months

Build the viewing platforms.
  • −200
  • Untouched Stratospheric Vents modifier removed
  • Stratospheric Vent Platforms modifier added
The resources are needed elsewhere.

Stratospheric Vent Expansion
Our engineers suggest that the underground gas reserves on [colony name] could be fully harnessed by expanding the vents with mining charges. The dangers of the vents collapsing should be negligible, but the local populace should be notified just in case.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Stratospheric Vents Studied
  • Colony has the Stratospheric Vent Refineries modifier
 Mean time to happen:

24 months

It is not worth the risk.
Set the charges!
The Guild will handle this.

Vent Expansion Success
The vent expansion project was a resounding success and our engineers are reporting increased gas production across the planet. As an unfortunate side-effect, some of the more fragile vents crashed spectacularly, causing minor damage to our facilities.

Despite this minor setback, we have managed to harness the unique conditions of [colony name] to great advantage.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Stratospheric Vent Expansion
  • 50% chance if not Mining Guilds
  • 75% chance if Mining Guilds
 Is triggered only by:

Chose Set the charges! or The Guild will handle this.

Excellent.
  • Stratospheric Vent Refineries modifier removed
  • Expanded Stratospheric Vents modifier added
  • The colony gains +10 Devastation

Vent Expansion Failure
Miscalculations with the mining charges caused several vents to fracture and eventually topple to the ground, resulting in considerable damage to our facilities. While some refineries are now producing more gas than before, the total yield has decreased. The silver lining is that the collapsed structures have revealed unexpected sources of minerals.

These events act as a reminder that galaxy-spanning civilizations cannot be built without cracking a few vents.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Stratospheric Vent Expansion
  • 50% chance if not Mining Guilds
  • 25% chance if Mining Guilds
 Is triggered only by:

Chose Set the charges! or The Guild will handle this.

Unfortunate.
  • Stratospheric Vent Refineries modifier removed
  • Collapsed Stratospheric Vents modifier added
  • The colony gains +20 Devastation
