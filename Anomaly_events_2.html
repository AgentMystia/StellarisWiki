Taking Stock
Please help with verifying or updating this infobox. It was last verified for version 3.11.

The below description is one of several available for this event.

The desert [planet name] turned out to be rather unremarkable.

While the crew monitored silent instruments, Science Officer [scientist name] was idly eyeing the [science ship name]'s energy reserves. According to their report the [science ship name] was issued less than the standard amount of energy when it last left port, but is more likely that [scientist name]'s carefree approach to resource management is to blame for the deficit.

Regardless, the ship does need additional Energy Credits.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Scientist has either the Perfectionist or the Intuition traits
  • Has at least 50 energy
 Is triggered only by:
  • Investigating Dunes anomaly

 Enabled if:

  • Scientist has Intuition trait
  • Has at least 75 energy
Typical.
  • Lose 75 energy

 Enabled if:

  • Scientist has Perfectionist trait
Unexpected windfall.
  • 6x energy output (100~1000)

Head in the Sand (anomaly.3060)
Please help with verifying or updating this infobox. It was last verified for version 3.11.

The below description is one of several available for this event.

Science Officer [scientist name] reports that the waves seen rippling across the sandy seas of [planet name] are, bizarrely enough, the result of active ship thrusters.

An alien vessel lies buried in the sand nose-down, its sputtering propulsion systems weakly agitating the dunes above. It appears to have been down there for some time, slowly driving itself deeper into the desert dunes. The [science ship name] lacks the means to excavate the struggling ship, and [scientist name] recommends sending a military vessel to [planet name] to unearth it.

Head in the Sand (anomaly.3061)
Please help with verifying or updating this infobox. It was last verified for version 3.11.
The [fleet name] eventually succeeds in raising the submerged alien vessel.

It is empty. Almost stripped bare, in fact.

Its crew must have abandoned ship before it was left to burrow into the particulate surface of [planet name], and they took most of the ship's non-critical internals with them. The engineers of the [fleet name] have managed to essentially hotwire the vessel, and have replenished its dwindling energy stores.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • None
 Is triggered only by:
  • Completing the Head in the Sand project, 33% base chance
A fresh commission for the [main species name] fleet.
