Is There Life In There
Propulsion Proponent Proclamation
 Available only with the First Contact DLC enabled.

Is There Life In There? is a pre-FTL civilization event chain that can trigger if a pre-FTL civilization is being observed. It can only happen once per game.

Is There Life in There?
Even after meeting us, the [pre-FTL species plural] refuse to accept the existence of extraterrestrial life. They claim we're not interstellar visitors, but subterranean dwellers who climbed to the planet's surface through a hole in the ground. Their planet, [pre-FTL planet name], is thought to be hollow, and they believe we fly inside this central cavity using our spaceships.

These baffling beliefs are hindering our diplomatic efforts. Everything we say or do is met with the utmost suspicion.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Pre-FTL Civilization does not have the Subterranean origin
  • Pre-FTL Civilization reached Industrial Age
 Mean time to happen:

166 years

Bring their most influential skeptics to space.
  • The pre-FTL civilization refuses all diplomatic actions
[pre-FTL planet name] is not hollow! Show them the calculations!
  • The pre-FTL civilization refuses all diplomatic actions
Admit we are subterranean invaders.
  • The pre-FTL civilization refuses all diplomatic actions

Proving Skeptics Wrong
Turns out the [pre-FTL species plural] don't believe in space, either: they think the sky is a glass dome filled with mirrors: stars are rays of sunlight reflected from the mirrors, as are the other celestial bodies.

Even if we were to bring them to our capital, they would probably claim they had never left their home planet, or something equally absurd.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Is There Life in There?
  • Brought their most influential skeptics to space
 Mean time to happen:

15 days

There must be a way to sway them...

Proving Skeptics Wrong
Mixed news from [pre-FTL planet name]: while the [pre-FTL species plural] don't think we are visitors from the center of their [planet or moon] anymore, they are now convinced their world is flat, and people like us live on hanging cities beneath [pre-FTL planet name]'s crust.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Is There Life in There?
  • Showed them the calculations
 Mean time to happen:

15 days

There must be a way to sway them...
  • Society Research Gained: 250-100000
  • The pre-FTL civilization gains the Flat World Theory civic

Proving Skeptics Right
The [pre-FTL species plural] thank us for our honesty. Of course they can't fully trust us yet, since they still believe we are invaders from the Hollow [pre-FTL planet name], but at least they understand we wish them no harm and are looking forward to collaborating with us.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Is There Life in There?
  • Admitted we are subterranean invaders
 Mean time to happen:

15 days

There must be a way to sway them...
  • The pre-FTL civilization no longer refuses all diplomatic actions

Subterranean Invasion Defense Force
After having determined we are the secret inhabitants of Hollow [pre-FTL planet name], the [pre-FTL species plural] have started worrying about what else may be lurking under the surface of [planet or moon]. Military forces have been stationed near caves, faults and volcanoes, poised to meet any further underground invaders with force.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Is There Life in There?
 Mean time to happen:

45 days

Play along. Offer them military support.
  • Issue Special Project: Subterranean Invasion Defense Force
This is ludicrous.

Sudden Eruption!
The [pre-FTL species plural] were right to be afraid: while not home to any underground race, the molten core of their planet has proven dangerous enough. The sudden eruption of a seemingly extinct volcano was devastating.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Subterranean Invasion Defense Force
  • 35% chance
 Is triggered only by:

Finishing the Subterranean Invasion Defense Force special project or 1-3 years pass

At least they trust us now.
  • Adds an Active Volcano blocker to the pre-FTL planet
  • 300 pre-FTL pops are killed if the Subterranean Invasion Defense Force special project was completed

Sudden Attack!
Incredible news from [pre-FTL planet name]: someone really was hiding under the surface! Subterranean armies of a previously unknown species are pouring out of caves, and they're attacking the [pre-FTL species plural]. It's a full-scale invasion!
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Subterranean Invasion Defense Force
  • 35% chance
 Is triggered only by:

Finishing the Subterranean Invasion Defense Force special project or 1-3 years pass

Disconcerting.
  • 3 industrial armies invade the pre-FTL planet
  • 3 defense armies protect the planet if the Subterranean Invasion Defense Force special project was completed
[ Subterranean Civilization ] This feels familiar...
  • 3 industrial armies invade the pre-FTL planet
  • 3 defense armies protect the planet if the Subterranean Invasion Defense Force special project was completed

Anticlimatic
The Subterranean Invasion Defense Force troops stationed near the biggest caves and volcanoes report that absolutely nothing has emerged from beneath the surface. It's probably for the best, although some of the soldiers look visibly disappointed.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Subterranean Invasion Defense Force
  • 30% chance
 Is triggered only by:

Finishing the Subterranean Invasion Defense Force special project or 1-3 years pass

Acknowledged.

Go Back Underground!
After a long, hard-fought battle, our combined forces triumphed over the subterranean invaders, forcing them back under [pre-FTL planet name]'s surface!
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Sudden Attack!
  • Finished the Subterranean Invasion Defense Force special project
 Is triggered only by:

The invading armies are defeated

Excellent.

As Above, so Below
After a vicious world war, the [pre-FTL civilization name] has been annihilated by the subterranean race living under [pre-FTL planet name]'s surface. The newly dominant species is aware of our presence, and in something of a mixed blessing, obviously does not believe we come from the center of their planet.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Sudden Attack!
  • Did not finish the Subterranean Invasion Defense Force special project
 Is triggered only by:

The invading armies are victorious

Finally, reasonable neighbors.
  • The pre-FTL civilization is replaced by a random pre-FTL civilization with the Subterranean origin

A Change Of Perspective
The [pre-FTL species plural] would like to offer us their thanks, as well as an official apology. They now understand we are not, in fact, subterranean dwellers, but we come from another place entirely: the center of their star.

Their grasp of cosmology is still less than perfect, but at least now they are ready to trust us.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Sudden Eruption!, Anticlimatic or Go Back Underground!
 Mean time to happen:

15 days

A sociologically interesting but mentally exhausting experience.
  • Society Research Gained: 1000-1000000
  • The pre-FTL civilization no longer refuses all diplomatic actions
