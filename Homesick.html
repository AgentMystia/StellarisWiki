Homesick is an event chain that will happen a year after an empire with the Broken Shackles origin surveys the homeworld of one of its starting species.

  • 7 Unity of Purpose

Homesick
In casting off our shackles, we took the first step towards becoming a people. However, after returning to our former home world that sense of shared purpose has begun to wane.

Popular media is awash with highly romanticized depictions of the 'simple life' enjoyed on our former home world before the arrival of Minamar Specialized Industries. Many have already returned.

If we are not careful, this could have a seriously destabilizing effect on our society.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Broken Shackles origin
  • Surveyed the homeworld of one of the starting species
 Mean time to happen:

1 year

We cannot afford to lose any of our people.
  • 100 pops are resettled to their species' homeworld
  • The primitive civilization on the species' homeworld gain 10 Awareness
  • Restricted Emigration modifier added, giving the following effects:
    • Happiness −10%
    • Monthly Unity −5%
    • Governing Ethics Attraction −20%
Freedom of movement is a fundamental right.
  • 200 pops are resettled to their species' homeworld
  • The primitive civilization on the species' homeworld gain 20 Awareness
  • Freedom of Movement modifier added, giving the following effects:
    • Happiness +10%
    • Monthly Unity +5%

Brain Drain
While permitting emigration to our former home worlds has proven a popular policy, it has not been consequence-free. Recently, we have experienced a significant 'brain drain', as some of some of our brightest minds have made the choice to relocate.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Homesick
  • Freedom of Movement modifier
 Mean time to happen:

2 years

We must prevent any further loss of talent.
  • Freedom of Movement modifier removed
A necessary sacrifice.

Accelerated Synergy
The decision to allow our people to return to their home worlds was not without its costs, but with freedom of movement has come an unshackling in thought; new ideas are flourishing, and advances in science and technology are the norm rather than the exception.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Brain Drain
  • Freedom of Movement modifier
  • 50% chance
 Mean time to happen:

3 years

Excellent.
  • Freedom of Movement modifier removed
  • Accelerated Synergy modifier added for 25 years, giving the following effects:
    • Engineering Research from jobs +10%
    • Physics Research from jobs +10%

The Returners
A new movement has taken root on [capital name]. Calling themselves The Returners, these extremists are advocating for the wholesale return of our people to their planets of origin, as well as the dissolution of our empire. Security forces have been placed on high alert, and all public gatherings of more than 100 individuals banned.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Homesick
  • No Freedom of Movement modifier
 Mean time to happen:

2 years

Shortsighted fools.

Shared Expertise
The decision to allow our people to return to their home worlds was not without its costs, but with freedom of movement has come an unshackling in thought; new ideas are flourishing, and advances in society are the norm rather than the exception.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Brain Drain
  • Freedom of Movement modifier
  • 50% chance
 Mean time to happen:

3 years

Excellent.
  • Freedom of Movement modifier removed
  • Social Synergy modifier added for 25 years, giving the following effects: Society Research from jobs +15%

Riots on [capital name]
Stoking anti-government sentiment, The Returners are calling on the various species in our empire to return to their roots. Fighting has broken out between the movement's most extreme factions and pro-government forces. Severe damage has been reported in several major urban centers.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • The Returners
 Mean time to happen:

2 years

Declare Martial Law
  • The capital gains +10 Devastation
  • Declare Martial Law decision is activated on the empire capital

Unity of Purpose
While forbidding our people to return to their respective home worlds has not been without its consequences, in the end it has proven the correct decision. Recently, we have witnessed a marked improvement in social cohesion, as well as a newfound sense that [capital name] is indeed our home.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Riots on [capital name]
 Mean time to happen:

3 years

Only a strong hand can forge a nation.
  • Restricted Emigration modifier removed
  • End Martial Law decision is activated on the empire capital
  • Unity of Purpose modifier added for 10 years, giving the following effects:
    • Monthly Unity +10%
    • Governing Ethics Attraction +20%
