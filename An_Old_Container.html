An Old Container
During maintenance operations aboard Arkship [arkship name], an unregistered, heavily reinforced container emitting radioactive signals was discovered on a lower deck. The lack of archival records or identifying markings leaves its origin and purpose unknown.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Not Gestalt Consciousness
 Mean time to happen:

10 years

Let's see what's inside.

An Unusual Finding
A basic scan showed the container held unremarkable goods and minerals, likely part of a standard supply shipment. However, lodged deeper within was a single, dense object with unusual properties. Its composition could not be determined.

The object reflected no energy signatures, absorbed most scanning wavelengths, and did not match any known materials in the ship's records. Even its exact shape proved difficult to define through remote imaging. Given these anomalies of its structure, some scientists of [arkship name] warned it could be potentially dangerous.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • An Old Container
  • 33% chance
 Mean time to happen:

6 months

Break the container open.
  • −500
Toss it out the airlock.
  • Unity Gained: 100-100000

A Valuable Find
A preliminary scan identified the bulk of the container's contents as remnants of a routine cargo manifest. However, buried beneath lay a cache of dense, crystalline alloys.

Further study of the radioactive emissions indicates the presence of Dark Matter within.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • An Old Container
  • 33% chance
 Mean time to happen:

6 months

Take everything that's inside.
  • Alloys Gained: 150-2000
  • Consumer Goods Gained: 100
  • Dark Matter Gained: 100

A Colorful Figure
Inside the container we identified the artifact of curious design. The object radiated faint but persistent energy signatures, fluctuating across multiple spectrums. Its form was geometrically astonishing, as if the shape itself resisted stable definition. It resembled a colorful figure, fashioned in the likeness of a living being. Its construction was intricate, composed of interlocking segments that suggest the figure is meant to be opened.

Some among the science crew suspect that something of value may rest within its outer shell. Others caution restraint, warning that tampering could compromise its preservation, or trigger unintended consequences.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • An Old Container
  • 33% chance
 Mean time to happen:

6 months

Open it.
  • −350
Leave it intact and put it on display.

A Veiled Crystal
The second container's casing resisted standard tools, requiring both time and resources to breach. But once inside, we discovered a dark and sharply faceted crystal, unlike anything documented in the ship's records. It emitted a faint warmth and absorbed ambient light, making it appear to radiate darkness from within. Its presence immediately drew attention among the crew.

Preliminary assessments by the science team suggested that, with proper study, the crystal might be harnessed as an energy source, though the method for doing so remains unclear.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • An Unusual Finding
  • Paid Energy
 Mean time to happen:

6 months

Display the crystal in the common area.
Determine the crystal's origin.
  • −1000
Investigate its energy-producing properties.
Display the crystal in the Grand Archive.

A Secret Within
Removing the artifact's outer shell revealed not a hidden device or cache of valuables, but another, smaller figure, crafted with the same uncanny detail.

When this second figure was opened, it too contained a smaller figure, and another within that. No mechanism or hidden inscription was uncovered, only a seemingly endless recursion of forms, as though the act of concealment itself was the purpose. The innermost figure was barely larger than a grain of sand, perfectly smooth, and heavy for its size.

Whatever meaning this held for its makers has been lost over time.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • A Colorful Figure
  • Paid Energy
 Mean time to happen:

6 months

A riddle for the ages.
  • The Riddle modifier added

Love of the Young
Preserving the outer shell of the figure and displaying it in one of our communal gathering halls, its intricate craftsmanship drew the attention of all who passed.

Strangely, the figure seemed to resonate most strongly with the young on Arkship [arkship name]. They often gathered around it, whispering theories about the being it depicts, or inventing their own stories. It appears to exert a calming influence, fostering imaginative play and easing anxiety.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • A Colorful Figure
  • Did not pay Energy
 Mean time to happen:

6 months

A remarkable companion.
  • Unity Gained: 150-100000

A Valuable Possession
The moment the crystal was unveiled, it began to pulse. It made no sound and showed no intention to communicate, yet its core thrummed when approached.

At intervals, it emitted a faint, fleeting glow. Some claimed it flickered in response to humming; others noted a subtle vibration in its cradle, as if it were listening.

No lifeform could be detected within, but it seemed aware of nearby presence. Strangest of all, those who glimpsed its glimmer often reported a quiet, inexplicable sense of joy.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • A Veiled Crystal
  • Did not pay Energy
 Mean time to happen:

6 months

A serene encounter.
  • Unity Gained: 100-100000
  • If empires doesn't have a Grand Archive module the event arkship gains +10% Happiness
  • If empires has a Grand Archive module it gains the Black Crystal specimen

A Crystallized Symphony
We have finally uncovered the crystal's origin. Through careful analysis of its pulse patterns, we discovered that the vibrations were not random at all. When the sequence was translated into sound, the tones matched an old lullaby, one still hummed aboard the Arkship today.

When the melody was played aloud, those who listened felt a deep, ancestral calm. The song was not just familiar; it was a remnant of life before the stars, perhaps even echoing from when we still called a planet home.

The crystal was placed in a communal space, its quiet pulse a living link to our shared origins.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • A Veiled Crystal
  • Paid Energy
 Mean time to happen:

6 months

What an endearing experience.
  • Physics Research Gained: 350-100000
