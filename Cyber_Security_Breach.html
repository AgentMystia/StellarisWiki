Cyber Security Breach is a brief event chain that has a chance to happen for all individualist empires that completed the Cyberization situation and finished the Cybernetics tradition tree. The event chain is completely linear, each event leading only to the next until the final choice. Depending on the choice made, it is possible to unlock a new leader trait.

Contents

  • 1 Cyber Security Breach
  • 2 Neuromancy
  • 3 Mind Jackers
  • 4 Rebooted; Renewed
  • 5 Neurological Firewalls

Cyber Security Breach
Recently, several classified documents were illegally downloaded by a remote server. Our security operatives are adamant that all primary and secondary firewalls remain intact, and so far there is no evidence of an external cyber attack.

The conclusion is inescapable: we have a leak, and given the sensitive nature of the stolen information, it must be someone with high-level clearance.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • 14.3% chance
 Mean time to happen:

12 months

Launch an investigation.
  • Leak Investigation modifier added, giving the following effects:
    • −5% Research Speed
    • −10% Cybernetic Worker Pop Resource Output
    • −15% Cybernetic Worker Happiness

Neuromancy
The investigation into our cyber security leak has concluded.

Certain augmented and high-level officials within the government have been targeted, their neurological wetware hacked. Manipulated by external agents and forced to act against their will, these individuals have no memory of the events in question.

Although we now know the cause of the leak, we are no closer to discovering who - or what - was ultimately responsible. However, by surreptitiously implanting false data into the minds of the affected officials, we may be able to trace the leak back to its source.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Cyber Security Breach
 Mean time to happen:

6 months

Understood.
  • 33% of the leaders with the Cyborg trait gain the Cyber Puppet trait, giving the following effects:
    • +20% Leader Upkeep
    • −20% Leader Experience Gain
    • +2 Leader Maximum Negative Traits
  • Puppets Among Us modifier added, giving the following effects:
    • −30 Stability
    • −15% Governing Ethics Attraction

Mind Jackers
We have them.

The criminals behind the unprecedented cyber attacks have been found. With no goal other than the sowing of chaos, these self-proclaimed 'Mind Jackers' managed to circumvent our security systems with embarrassing ease. While the ringleaders are already in custody, we must still decide what to do with our compromised officials.

The simplest solution is to install a custom firewall around their affected implants. A cleaner, though more risky, option is a hard reboot: accessing their base cognitive functions, the leaders in question could be forcibly 'shut down' while a new operating system is installed.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Neuromancy
 Mean time to happen:

12 months

Build the firewall.
  • Leak Investigation modifier removed
Reboot the officials.
  • Leak Investigation modifier removed
They're onto us. Build the wall.
  • Leak Investigation modifier removed

Rebooted; Renewed
The hard reboot of our compromised officials has resulted in some unexpected consequences: every trace of the neurological hack has been wiped clean, but so too has a great portion of their accumulated knowledge and experience.

Despite this setback, in 'starting fresh' these leaders report deep feelings of professional and personal renewal. By forcing us to take such drastic measures, the hackers may have unintentionally done us a favor.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Mind Jackers
  • No firewall
 Mean time to happen:

3 months

Understood.
  • Puppets Among Us modifier removed
  • All leaders with the Cyber Puppet trait lose all negative traits and 300 experience for each trait lost

Neurological Firewalls
Our minds are safe.

Wetware engineers have successfully designed a neurological firewall. In order to maintain our collective security, the program will be immediately installed in all current and future officials. While this will have a detrimental impact on the base cognitive functionality of our leadership class, the project has provided significant insight into the science of data security.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Mind Jackers
  • Firewall
 Mean time to happen:

12 months

Safety first.
  • Puppets Among Us modifier removed
  • Resolved Vigilance modifier added for 5 years, giving the following effects: +25% Governing Ethics Attraction
  • All leaders with the Cyber Puppet trait lose the trait
  • All leaders with the Cyborg trait gain the Neurologial Firewall trait
  • Gain a random Computing technology or a large amount of Physics Research if all technologies were researched
