Genesis

The new species going through history on the other side of the astral rift

Genesis is a unique rift that will create a new species with pre-sapient traits. Genesis can only spawn once per galaxy. It cannot spawn for empires that do not have at least one colony. Every chapter has a difficulty of 3 and the following rewards:

  1. small astral threads reward (40, 50, or 60).
    1. Arm them with might gives the species the Strong trait
    2. Plant the seeds of knowledge gives the species the Natural Intellectuals trait.
    3. We play no games. Activate an E.M.P. ends the exploration and grants 48x alloys output (700~30 000) and large astral threads reward (140, 150, or 160).
  2. small astral threads reward (40, 50, or 60).
    1. Diligence gives the species the Proles trait.
    2. Intuition gives the species the Ingenious trait.
  3. small astral threads reward (40, 50, or 60).
    1. Preserve the old ways gives the species the Conservative trait.
    2. Challenge every frontier gives the species the Starborn trait.
  4. large astral threads reward (140, 150, or 160). On a random colony, a pop from the new species is created with the matching climate preference. If the empire is Gestalt Consciousness, it also receives 24x society output (500~1 000 000) and the Genesis Insight permanent empire modifier.
Available only with the Grand Archive DLC enabled.

Finishing the astral rift grants the Genesis Core specimen
