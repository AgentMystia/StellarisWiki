Template:Yellow light/doc

Makes text yellow, as the game uses for neutral effects and for emphasis. The background color is set to black to increase visibility when infoboxes aren't working. Use no_background to disable the background color

Usage

{{yellow light|text you want yellow}} gives
text you want yellow
{{yellow light|text you want yellow|no_background=yes}} gives
text you want yellow
