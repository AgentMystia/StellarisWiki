A collection

The collection contains every specimen obtained by an empire. Specimens can be obtained from events or discoveries. Specimens can be displayed if the empire has the Galactic Archivism technology and a Grand Archive megastructure, which provides various benefits but also has an Energy upkeep which is increased by Empire Size. Hovering over a specimen will also display the date it was collected. Specimens can be sold for Energy and the storage can be set to automatically sell specimens of a certain rarity.

Each empire can store up to 9 specimens ready for exhibition from each category and can store an additional 18 specimens of any category. If this storage is full, any newly collected specimens will be sold. Specimens can be stolen if the starbase of the system containing the Grand Archive megastructure is occupied, which will also destroy the megastructure. One specimen from each category will be stolen, or two if the attacking empire has the Barbaric Despoilers civic.

At least one specimen must be obtained for the Galactic Archivism technology to appear as a research option, with increased likelyhood for each specimen collected.

Specimens can be traded with other empires, with trade acceptance depending on the rarity of the specimen. If a specimen is obtained via trade, obtaining it normally will not give another copy.
