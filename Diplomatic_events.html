Diplomatic events have a small chance to trigger every 5 years as long as an Official is assigned to a Federation or the Galactic Community. Each event can happen multiple times per game but no more than once every 15 years. They can also be triggered by the Spark Diplomatic Incident operation.

Scandalous Insult
We have caught a delegate from the [alien empire name] in a rare blunder, letting slip their empire's true feelings about us to another, anonymous party. More than the usual political mud-slinging: these amount to an outright insult, as it appears we are the subjects of fierce ridicule amongst [alien empire adjective] society.

Whether or not we pay any mind to this baseless slander: diplomacy and pride would demand that we stand our ground.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Official assigned to Federation or Galactic Community
 Mean time to happen:

60 months

This is an outrage!
  • −60 Opinion with another empire
  • If both empires are in the same Federation it loses 15 Cohesion
  • If one empire is the other's subjects it loses 15 Loyalty
We are above such antics. Ignore them.
  • Influence: −10
They are not worthy of our scorn.
  • Influence: −10

Substance Abuse
Our ambassador corps has for some time been concerned at the performance of [official name], one of our officials to the [Galactic Community or federation name]. Now, evidence has come to light that [he/she/they] appear[s] to have fallen prey to an alien substance from the [alien name] that has proven extremely addictive to [founder species plural].

Although the trade in this substance is highly illegal among the [alien species plural], a lucrative black market run by cutthroat drug lords has sprung up. These drug lords, who apparently managed to introduce it to [him/her/them] as a potent aphrodisiac, now exert a considerable degree of influence over [official name], who is forced to do their bidding in return for satisfying [his/her/their] cravings.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Official assigned to Federation or Galactic Community
  • Not Gestalt Consciousness
  • Not Mechanical founder species
 Mean time to happen:

60 months

Fire [official name].
  • Diplomatic Corps Ridiculed modifier added for 120 months, giving the following effects: −15% Diplomatic Weight
Recall [him/her/them] to [capital name] for retraining.
  • The official is unassigned and gains the Substance Abuser trait

Astute Galactic Diplomacy
Although we are not able to count ourselves among the true superpowers of this galaxy, we have often been able to punch above our weight in galactic affairs. This is largely down to the exertions of our official [official name], who ably represents our interests in the Galactic Community and commands deep respect even among those stridently opposed to all the [empire name] stands for. As a result, even though not everyone may fear the might of our navy or long to match the depths of our coffers, they nevertheless admire the prowess of our diplomatic corps.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Official over 60 years old assigned to Galactic Community
  • Not part of the Galactic Council
  • Among the top 5 empires by Diplomatic Weight
 Mean time to happen:

60 months

Excellent.
  • Diplomatic Corps Respected modifier added for 120 months, giving the following effects: +10% Diplomatic Weight

Lobbyists
A cacophony of voices may be heard in the Galactic Community - not all of them friendly, and certainly not all of them aligning with [empire adjective] interests. Unfortunately, it seems that Ambassador [official name] has been listening to the wrong voices. Eyebrows were raised when [he/she/they] attended several conferences hosted by the [faction name]. Now it has emerged that a large sum of credits traceable to that organization were transferred to [him/her/them].

[official name]'s colleagues demand that [he/she/they] be recalled, citing trust issues. However, some of our more devious advisors suggest that we could strike a deal to keep [him/her/them] in place in return for a share of the pot.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Official assigned to Galactic Community
  • Not Gestalt Consciousness
 Mean time to happen:

60 months

What could be the harm in it?
  • Energy Credits Gained: 100-1000
  • Beholden to Special Interests modifier added for 120 months, giving the following effects: −15% Diplomatic Weight
Recall [him/her/them] to [capital name] for retraining.
  • The official is unassigned and gains the Corrupt trait

A Contribution to Galactic Comedy
[official name], our official representing our interests in the Galactic Community, has committed a major faux pas, mistaking the [council member empire adjective] delegate for a [inferior empire species name]. Unfortunately, the incriminating remarks were witnessed by many onlookers, who duly showered [official name] with ridicule. Many now cast aspersions on the competence of our diplomatic corps, although our contribution to galactic comedy cannot be doubted.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Official assigned to Galactic Community
  • Galactic Council was created
  • Not part of the Galactic Council
  • Not in a Federation or Defensive Pact with all Galactic Council members
  • Not Gestalt Consciousness
 Mean time to happen:

60 months

So be it.
  • Diplomatic Corps Ridiculed modifier added for 120 months, giving the following effects: −10% Diplomatic Weight
Recall [him/her/them] to [capital name] for retraining.
  • The official is unassigned and gains the Melancholy trait
Recall [him/her/them] to [capital name] for reconditioning.
  • The official is unassigned and gains the Chemically Misaligned trait
Recall it to [capital name] for reprogramming.
  • The official is unassigned and gains the Glitchy trait

Disputes Among Allies
The recent law changes in the [federation name] caused a significant stir, with old ideological debates between ourselves and the [opposing ethics empire name] once again rising to the fore. Our representative, [official name], was able to persuade many of the benefits of a more [empire ethic] outlook, highlighting the cowardice and naivety inherent in the beliefs of the [opposing ethics empire name] official, [opposing ethics empire official name]. Fortunately, the [opposing ethics empire founder species] took this in good grace, recognizing the need for compromise in the interest of a more harmonious relationship with their alliance partners.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Official assigned to Federation
  • Another Federation member has opposing ethics
  • Not Gestalt Consciousness
 Mean time to happen:

60 months

A Strange Creature
[official name], our official to the Galactic Community, has been spending a disturbing amount of time tending to a strange, small mammalian creature that began following [him/her/them] around. Presumably a domestic pet that escaped from one of the other delegations, it is undeniably cute but shows no sign of sapient thought. Although it is highly carnivorous and exhibits sharp teeth and claws, it is generally affectionate and desires plentiful physical contact in the form of petting - though its mood swings can be sudden and violent!

Although the creature has proved a distraction for [official name]'s more serious tasks, other delegates have commented on the endearing nature of the softer, more sentimental side of the [founder species name] psyche that has been revealed.
 
Trigger conditions:
  • Official of the founder species assigned to Galactic Community
  • Founder species does not use a human portrait
  • Not Gestalt Consciousness
  • United Nations of Earth or Commonwealth of Man are members of the Galactic Community
  • Not in Federation, Overlord or Subject of United Nations of Earth or Commonwealth of Man
  • Among the top 5 empires by Diplomatic Weight
 Mean time to happen:

60 months

Perfect.
  • +50 Opinion with United Nations of Earth, Commonwealth of Man and any Xenophile empire that joined the Galactic Community
