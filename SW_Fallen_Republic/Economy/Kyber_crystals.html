Summary

Kyber crystals are rare, highly reactive, green crystals with the unique characteristic of beeing able to absorb energy and emit it at a way more powerful intesity. One Blaster shot into a Crystal as big as a Human is potent enough to blow up a cruiser. They where famously used in the production of super laser technology. Holding their production a secret was at the utmost priority for the Empire in order to ensure the secrecy about the Deathstar Project.

Preqesitions

In order to gather Kyber Crystals in Star Wars Fallen Rebublic the player needs two prerequisites

  1. Starting as the Empire
  2. Research the Kyber Mining Tech

Kyber Sources

There are 2 ways to gather Kyber Crystals in the Star Wars Fallen Republic Mod

Space Mining Stations

  • Kyber Deposits spawn with a size of 1 to 3
  • Kyber Deposits can appear on frozen Planets and inside Nebulas
    • On frozen Planets the drop weight is
      • 0.05 for 1 Kyber
      • 0.02 for 2 Kybers
      • 0.01 for 3 Kybers

Planetary Mine

  • It requieres a Kyber Crystal Vein Planetary Feature
    • On such a Feature a Kyber Mine can be build
      • One Mine offers one Worker Job that produces 6 Kyber Crystals and has an upkeep of 0.5 Credits
    • There is a 0.1 Drop Weight that a new Habitable World has a Kyber Crystal Vein

Uses for Kyber Crystals

The current sole use of Kyber Crystals is to complete one Step of the DS-1 Orbitalbattlestation Megastructure construction that will require 2500 Kyber Crystals

Strategy and Tips

Getting a lot of Kybers in a short amount of time to construct the Death Star early on is challanging. Especially with a low setting to habitable worlds chances are slim to get a vein, a very good source of Kyber.

  • The Player should look out for sources early on, even tho a mining station may not be buildable the sources can be seen
    • This should be taken into consideration for expansions as the Empire
  • The Kyber Vein of Christophsis and therefore the Confederate Remnants should always taken into consideration if one wants to get Kyber as fast as possible
  • Else scouting through the Planetary Features for Colonisable Worlds should be done
  • The maximum storing capacity for Kybers is 5000
  • Kyber Crystals can not be traded on the Market
