Specialization districts

These districts can be built on any colony with 20 or more Pops by adding them with the appropriate Planetary Decision for 100. Two of these decisions can be made and each allows for 5 districts of their type. This also adds +5 Max Districts. They can also be abolished for 50 with the appropriate Planetary Decision
