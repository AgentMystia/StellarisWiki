Slave Trade [edit]
A wretched but profitable industry, slavery has been with the galaxy since the beginning, and shows no signs of ending.
Requirements: Slaver Guilds and Intergalactic Banking Clan, Commerce Guild, Confederate Remnant, Trade Federation, Corporate Alliance, Tion Hegemony, Galactic Empire, Hapes Consortium, Republic Loyalists, File:RebelAllianceIcon.png Rebel Alliance, File:MonCalaMonarchyIcon.png Mon Cala Monarchy, or Mandalorian Clans

Adoption effect: +10% Slave Output
+10% Planet Build Speed

−10% Building upkeep
−10% Starbase upkeep
+10% Planet Build Speed
+15% Ship Build Speed

+5% Specialist Pop Resource Output
+20% Governing Ethics Attraction
−15% Slave Upkeep
+10 Stability
Finisher effect: +1 Soldier Job per 20 Pops
