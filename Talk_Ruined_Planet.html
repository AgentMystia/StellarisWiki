The original information from the Astral Rifts page is included below, unmodified:

Ruined Planet

The ruined planet on the other side of the astral rift

The Ruined Planet astral rift is always present in the home system of empires with the Riftworld origin. It has the following chapters:

1. Ruined Planet: "Search for remaining survivors" (narrative)
2. Opposing Factions, choice:
  • "Visit the Black Curtain" (available if Genocidal)
  • "Visit the Starlight Vanguard"
3. Lost Anchestors, choice:
  • "The flow of time is vastly different here..."
  • "We were engineered?"
4. The Black Curtain (if chosen to visit), choice:
  • 3 "Offer [resource]." , pay
    1000 basic resources
    1000 food if biological
    1000 minerals if Lithoid
    1000 energy if Machine
    , proceed to World Council
  • 5 "Offer astral thread." , pay 100 astral threads, proceed to World Council
  • 1 "These efforts are hopeless. Let us visit the militants.", proceed to The Starlight Vanguard (available if did not visit the Starlight Vanguard)
  • 1 "This is a lost cause. Let us return to the new world.", proceed to Abandoning Our Ancestors (available if visited the Starlight Vanguard)
4. The Starlight Vanguard (if chosen to visit), choice:
  • 3 "Offer alloys.", pay 100 alloys, proceed to World Council
  • 5 "Offer astral thread.", pay 100 astral threads, proceed to World Council
  • 1 "This war is hopeless. Let us visit the preservationists.", proceed to The Black Curtain (available if did not visit the Black Curtain and Genocidal)
  • 1 "This is a lost cause. Let us return to the new world", proceed to Abandoning Our Ancestors (available if visited the Black Curtain or Genocidal)
5. World Council
  • 3 "Offer astral thread.", pay 100 astral threads
  • 3 "Offer energy.", pay 1000 energy,
  • 6 "Offer nothing."
Regardless of choice: on success proceed to Celebration, on failure proceed to Devastation
5. Abandoning our Ancestors, gain large astral threads reward (140, 150, or 160) and a small amount of Unity. Ends the rift.
6. Celebration, choice:
  • "Recruit scientist.", gain a level 3 Scientist with the Rift Warped trait
  • "Recruit commander.", gain a level 3 Commander with the Rift Warped trait
  • "Recruit official.", gain a level 3 Official with the Rift Warped trait
Regardless of choice, proceed to Spirit of our Ancestors
6. Devastation, choice:
  • "Take as many back as we can.", gain Unity
  • "Accept the noble's offer.", gain Energy and an age 50 Official that cannot gain negative traits.
  • "Preserve the library.", gain Research and Unity. (available if Genocidal)
  • "Claim their knowledge.", gain Research and Unity. (available if Genocidal)
Regardless of choice, proceed to Spirit of our Ancestors
7. Spirit of our Ancestors, gain a unique empire modifier with the following effects:
Helped Black Curtain Helped Starlight Vanguard
+5% Pop Growth Speed
+5% Habitability 		+10% Kinetic Weapon Damage
  • Gave astral threads:
    • +25% Astral Rift Exploration Speed
    • −50% Astral Rift Fail Probability
  • Gave resources:
    • +0.5 Food from Farmers (if biological)
    • +0.5 Minerals from Miners (if Lithoid)
    • +0.5 Energy from Technicians (if Machine)
  • Gave astral threads:
    • +10% Shield Hit Points
    • +10% Shield Hardening
  • Gave alloys:
    • +10% Armor Hit Points
    • +10% Armor Hardening
World Council
  • Gave astral threads:
    • +5% Sublight Speed
  • Gave energy:
    • +5% Ship Build Speed

One year after the astral rift is completed (regardless of outcome), the Formless contacts the empire and hint at the Crystal Rift requirements. (see "The Crystal Rift")
