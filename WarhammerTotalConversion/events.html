Main

In this section I want to describe every event that I made for this mod. There are 3 types of events: Lore, random and special. Lore events is the same that occurred in Warhammer 40k and are official for example Armageddon wars or Gothic war. Random events are (as the name suggest) random. For example ork invasion or warpstorm. It would be too easy to play this mod if you know every place that will be attacked. Special events are for example alliance formed at the start of the game or starting fleet and army.

If you don't want to be spoiled and make galaxy burn don't read this ;)

Chaos events

Sometimes chaos cult can arise on your planet . You will be able to send Inquisitor to investigate these and a random outcome will be crated. Exterminatus, elimination of the chaos cult or Inquisitor being killed.

Warp Storms will sometimes spawn in one of your systems. You will be unable to fly through it and unable to reach the planet affected. Stay vigilant because warpstorm can change planets into demon worlds and there is no way to destroy them(yes exterminatus is not enough)

Tyranids events

Tyranid fleets will come to your galaxy after (200 years). This fleet will orbit the closest colonized planed and after destroying fortifications they will invade it with their armies. If Tyranids win the planet will be changed into barren wasteland. After that the fleet will fly to the next colonized planet. This behavior is looped so to stop the tyranids you have to destroy their fleet.
