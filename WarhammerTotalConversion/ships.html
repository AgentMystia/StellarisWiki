Special ships

There are some special ships impossible to build and available only throught events. Some races have them some not. The ships are:

  • Eternal Cruseder
  • Phalanx
  • Craftworld
  • Blackstone fotress
  • Planet Killer
  • Space Hulk Ship
  • Ships from the past - battleship with technology not available now. For example Imperium with batteship that have lv5 mass drivers. They are unable to reproduce id and use lv 2 mass drivers in all newly produced ships.
  • Necron "planet destroyer" in the future
