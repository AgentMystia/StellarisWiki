Human Ground Forces

Overview

All 7 Imperial Factions can build all the below units when meeting all the required criteria. Certain Imperial Armies require particular planets and planet types so they can be built. The Imperial units range from the elite Space Marine Companies to the Conscript Regiments used as cannon fodder. As well as this titan war machines are also buildable capable of destroying entire invasions by themselves. One thing is certain with all Imperial troops they will all fight in the name of the Emperor and die in his service. Whether they die by the enemies hand or by Imperial firing squads is another matter.

Lore

After the Horus Hersey to avoid a rebellion of that scale again the Imperial Army was dissolved. in its place it was replaced by the Imperial Guard and the Imperial Navy both completely separate from each other. The Imperial Guard form the bulk of the Imperial ground forces. Numerous Imperial Guard regiments fight in thousands of battles across the galaxy per hour in service to their emperor, However with these standard regiments come the more elite and specialised regiments and forces. Formed from worlds which are inhospitable or warzones they are battle hardened soldiers. From the Cadian Shock Troops to the Catachan Jungle fighters they are more deadlier than there standard counterparts. However the pinnacle of the Imperium's soldiers are the Space Marines. Genetically engineered super soldiers whose one purpose is to kill the enemy in the most badass fashion. Space Marines are one of if not the best warriors in the galaxy. When discussing the Imperium's weapons of war none beat the Titan war machines few in number but able to bring devastating firepower to the battlefield. These gigantic weapons put even the toughest Ork Waaaagh to the test. When you play one of the Imperial factions you can be sure you will have a variety of troops to use.

Eldar Craftworld Forces

Overview

Eldar Troops are well trained, experienced and deadly. Able to match most other nations in terms of firepower. As well as this Eldar titans are also as powerful as most imperial ones. However Eldar units tend to be expensive and high in maintenance. However such a professional army will have its costs.

Lore

The Eldar are one of the oldest races in the universe and also the most deadliest. Eldar craftworlds are full with well trained Eldar soldiers. They are brutally efficient when killing their enemies. Eldar weaponry is also very advanced giving them another edge on the battle field. However I would pick a good old lasgun any day instead of an Eldar weapon.

Ork Boyz

Overview

The Orks are tough and blood Thirsty on the ground able to attack you with the full power of the horde. Ork infantry Armies tend to be better than other nations average units. Ork titans are poorly made so aren't as powerful as other nations ones. However Ork titans are cheaper so you may be facing more than a couple. The Ork army is truly powerful because of the bonus their race gets. Nearly all the Ork genetics and starting national traits are to do with greater combat efficiency. Making an Ork invasion a nightmare to other races

Lore

Orks were developed to be a weapon and that aim was achieved. Ork society is run on a tribal basis with the biggest and strongest Warboss taking charge. Orks live to fight and so are very good at it. When a big enough target is found the Orks will launch a Waaaagh to kill and plunder everything there. Ork invasions are tough to beat are usually very costly to both sides. Orks are without a doubt one of the few races which left unchecked could end your game quickly.

Tau Fire Caste

Overview

The Tau Army is weak compared to other factions in the game. They wont be able to match the Imperium of Man on the ground in terms of firepower. The Tau get access to both their Own Fire Warriors and Kroot and Vespid Allies. The pinnacle of the Tau ground force is the Broadside Battle suit and the riptide Battle suit. Cheap and effective the Tau can still put up a good fight. Note that Tau units are generally quite cheap.

Lore

The Tau Empire are one of the newer races of the galaxy and their ground forces are made up of members from the warrior Fire Caste. By the 41st millennium they became a power in their own right. However the Tau have always lacked s strong melee force meaning Tau armies tend to rip you apart from a distance. The Tau Military is advanced with state of the art battle suits giving entire regiments a run for their money. Plus the Kroot eat you so be careful of that.

Forces of the Chaos Gods

Overview

Chaos one of the most dangerous factions within the game. From their daemon planets the dark Mechanicum build thousands of war weapons. A Chaos player will be able to choose from Cultists to daemons all deadly in different ways. Chaos also have access to chaos space marines who are just as deadly as their imperial counterparts. Chaos forces on the whole have a strong army. Like the Imperial brethren Chaos also have access to Titans. They have become the twisted versions of the imperial variants. the crew have melded with the machine which is now possessed by a daemon.

Lore

The Chaos Army is extremely strong and able to build quickly on their daemon planets. However Chaos soldiers also tend to go insane meaning they are not as disciplined as the Imperial forces. Although the Chaos army is not to be underestimated. After the Horus Hersey the Chaos armies fled into their warp storms. Chaos forces only amass when Abaddon the Despoiler calls a Black Crusade. You can be sure that until judgement is delivered on eye of terror Cadia will forever be a warzone.

Dark Eldar Strike Force

Overview

The Dark Elder have no coherent armed forces. Their military is made up of lightly armed and quick moving troops. However the Dark Elder excel at striking where you least expect it. The Dark Elder are not renown fighters. Compared to the Imperium their ground forces are weak. This is a major weakness for the Dark Elder meaning the only strategy would be quick striking armies invading where your enemies are least expecting it. Using your navy will be the key to beating more powerful ground units

Lore

The Dark Eldar are a nation of Eldar turned to evil. They lust for power and slaves. This led to the Dark Eldar's ground forces being comprised mainly of raiding parties The aim of these raids are to acquire slaves for their despicable pleasures. However one must never underestimate the Dark Eldar or you might end up a slave yourself.

Necron Tomb Worlds

Overview

A Necron army never sleeps, never goes hungry and never surrenders. Necron troops and vehicles and very powerful using gauss weaponry far superior to most other races. Necron troops are tough and are able to rip through many battle lines before being stooped. However Necrons only have access to one type of titan the monolith although it can go toe to toe with a emperor class titan. Necron armies tend to be large and deadly. My advice would be kill them in space before they can land.

Lore

Necrons used to be humanoid until the C 'tan tricked them into becoming mindless servants to their every will. Every single Necron used to be a man, women or child. They are over 65 million years old kept alive as metal automatons. Necrons are very deadly from the scarabs that rip the flesh of their prey to the Monolith capable of destroying entire armies. One things is for sure few things will stop a Necron advance.

The Tyranid Swarm

Overview

The Tyranid swarm have access to a number of creatures that will make your like a misery. Their basic troops are relatively weak however come in large numbers however they reproduce quickly. Although Tyranid Warriors are able to go toe to toe with even the largest of Imperial Guard Regiments. Tyranids also have access to bio-titans which are more that a match for imperial titans. Even the most experienced players may face a challenge when facing the swarm.

Lore

Tyranids where first encountered in the 41st millennium by the imperium of man. Names after the planet they were first discovered on Tyran. Three hive fleets have entered the galaxy since first contact each causing widespread death and leave hundreds of worlds lifeless. The swarm have an endless number troops to throw at their opponents. From Hormagaunts to Hierophants they form an endless wave of destruction. Tyranid armies are connected by synapse creatures making them extremely deadly. Be prepared when facing the Tyranids.

Armies

All require the Assault Armies Technology. These units are the bread and butter of the swarm. with the combination of extremely cheap costs and low maintenance fees it means worlds are usually overrun by tyranid armies in a matter of days
