Recruitment Buildings

Each race has certain armies which require recruitment buildings. For example to build the raider titan as the Dark Eldar you must have built the raider recruitment building on that planet.


Chaos and Imperial Titans require the base buildings so they can be constructed. For example the imperial knight requires the imperial knight base.


Space Marines and their Chaos counterparts require the fortress monastry to be constructed before they can be built.


Recruitment and base buildings have varying costs depending on the unit it produces.
